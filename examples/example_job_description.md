## Senior Software Engineer - Payments Platform

**Company:** FinFlow Technologies  
**Location:** Remote (US/EU)  
**Type:** Full-time

### About Us

FinFlow is a fast-growing fintech startup (Series B, $45M raised) building the next generation of payment infrastructure. We process $2B+ annually and serve 500+ enterprise clients.

### The Role

We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Payments Platform team. You'll design and build critical financial infrastructure handling millions of transactions daily.

### Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable payment processing systems
- Build APIs that handle 10,000+ requests per second
- Ensure 99.99% uptime for mission-critical financial services
- Mentor junior engineers and lead technical design reviews
- Collaborate with product and compliance teams on new features

### Requirements

- 5+ years of software engineering experience
- Strong experience with Python, Go, or Java
- Experience with distributed systems and microservices
- Database expertise (PostgreSQL, Redis)
- Understanding of payment systems, PCI compliance a plus
- Experience with AWS or GCP cloud infrastructure

### Nice to Have

- Previous fintech or payments experience
- Experience with event-driven architectures (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- Kubernetes and containerization experience
- Track record of mentoring engineers

### What We Offer

- Competitive salary ($180K - $220K) + equity
- Remote-first culture
- Health, dental, vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO
- Learning & development budget
