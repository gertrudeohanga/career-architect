### Metric Types to Include

- **Performance**: Response times, throughput, uptime percentages
- **Scale**: Users, transactions, data volume
- **Efficiency**: Time saved, cost reduction, productivity gains
- **Business**: Revenue impact, customer satisfaction, conversion rates

### Modern Builder Verbs

Use these instead of generic verbs:

- "Locked architectural intent" instead of "designed"
- "Constrained entropy" instead of "maintained"
- "Steered AI exploration" instead of "used AI"
- "Improved decision throughput" instead of "increased velocity"
- "Delivered system durability" instead of "built stable system"
