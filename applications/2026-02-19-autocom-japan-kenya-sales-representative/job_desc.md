# Autocom Japan Kenya - Sales Representative

## Company Overview

Autocom Japan Kenya Ltd is a growing automotive trading company specializing in sourcing and selling quality vehicles from Japan to the Kenyan market. The company focuses on delivering reliable vehicles, transparent processes, and excellent customer service to both individual and corporate clients.

## Role Summary

Sales Representative responsible for generating leads, converting prospects into customers, and building long-term client relationships in the automotive import sector. This is an immediate-start position based in Nairobi or Mombasa with a focus on selling Japanese vehicles to the Kenyan market.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identify and pursue new sales opportunities through cold calling, walk-ins, referrals, and online inquiries
- Present and promote company vehicles and services to prospective clients
- Understand customer needs and recommend suitable vehicle options
- Follow up on leads and maintain a strong sales pipeline
- Negotiate pricing and close deals professionally
- Maintain accurate records of client interactions and sales activities
- Provide excellent after-sales support to ensure customer satisfaction
- Meet or exceed monthly sales targets
- Stay informed about current vehicle inventory, pricing, and market trends
- Represent the company professionally at all times

## Required Qualifications

- Diploma or bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, Commerce, or related fields
- 2–3 years in sales, preferably in automotive, retail, or customer-facing industries
- Strong communication and negotiation skills
- Confident, self-motivated, and target-oriented
- Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
- Basic computer skills (WhatsApp, email, spreadsheets, CRM tools are a plus)
- Willingness to learn about vehicle specifications and import processes
- Availability to start immediately
- Must be based in or willing to work in Nairobi or Mombasa

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in automotive sales or vehicle import industry
- Knowledge of Japanese vehicle market and specifications
- Existing client network in automotive sector
- CRM system experience

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Communication:** WhatsApp, Email
- **Productivity:** Spreadsheets (Excel/Google Sheets)
- **CRM:** Basic CRM tools (preferred but not required)
- **Other:** Basic computer literacy required

## Compensation & Benefits

- **Salary Range:** USD 300 fixed monthly salary
- **Commission:** Performance-based commissions and bonuses
- **Benefits:** On-the-job training and product knowledge support
- **Growth:** Opportunity to grow within a fast-expanding company
- **Environment:** Supportive team environment
