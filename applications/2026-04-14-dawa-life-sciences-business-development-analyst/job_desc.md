# Dawa Life Sciences - Business Development Analyst

## Company Overview

Dawa Life Sciences is a pharmaceutical company operating in Kenya's healthcare sector. The company focuses on expanding its presence in the pharmaceutical industry through strategic business development and market expansion initiatives.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Analyst will be part of the Business Development team, playing a crucial role in driving revenue growth, fostering innovation, and expanding the company's presence in the pharmaceutical industry through strategic partnerships, market research, and client relationship management.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identifying and pursuing new business opportunities through market research and analysis to identify potential target markets, customers, and partnerships
- Actively seeking out new collaborations, licensing agreements, and strategic alliances to expand the company's product portfolio and market research
- Building and maintaining strong relationships with key stakeholders, including clients, healthcare organizations, regulatory authorities, and partners
- Engaging in effective negotiation and contract management to secure new business deals and ensure favorable terms
- Analyzing market trends, competition, and industry developments to develop strategic plans and recommendations for business growth
- Serving as the primary contact for all key client matters, ensuring timely and accurate communication and addressing any issues swiftly
- Working closely with internal teams, including sales, marketing and healthcare teams, to deliver solutions and achieve mutual success

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor of Science degree in Biotechnology and Biomedical Sciences, Business, Pharmacy or any other related field
- Strong numerical proficiency, logical reasoning and problem-solving skills
- Strong communication, negotiation and interpersonal skills
- Ability to build and maintain strong relationships
- Experience in the pharmaceutical industry, particularly in sales and key accounts management
- Strategic thinking and problem-solving skills
