# Moja EV Kenya LTD - Business Development Executive

## Company Overview

Moja EV Kenya LTD operates in the Energy sector, focusing on electric vehicle solutions in Kenya. The company is part of the growing EV ecosystem in East Africa, contributing to sustainable transportation and clean energy adoption.

## Role Summary

Business Development Executive responsible for driving sales of electric vehicles, managing customer relationships, conducting market research, and achieving sales targets in Nairobi. This role combines sales expertise with customer service excellence and market intelligence gathering.

## Key Responsibilities

- Customer Reception and Consultation for EV products
- Sales of Car Products (Electric Vehicles)
- Sales Negotiation and Promotion
- Provide After-Sales Service
- Achieve Sales Targets
- Exceptional customer service skills, including interpersonal communication and rapport building
- Coordinate Internal market research and Competitive Analysis
- Negotiation skills to offer deals that satisfy while ensuring customers profitability

## Required Qualifications

- More than 5 years of sales or marketing experience
- Strong initiative, positive work attitude, passion for car sales work
- Strong sense of career development, willingness to face challenges
- Good communication and expression skills
- Adaptability and problem-solving ability
- Good mental resilience
- Good teamwork spirit and customer service awareness
- Basic computer skills such as word processing, Excel spreadsheets and PowerPoint presentations
- Proficient driving ability, holding a valid driver's license for more than two years
- Diploma or Associate's degree
- Proficient in English

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in automotive sales
- Knowledge of electric vehicle technology
- Understanding of Kenya's automotive market
- Experience with market research and competitive analysis

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Software:** Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint
- **CRM:** Customer relationship management systems
- **Skills:** Sales negotiation, market research, customer service, after-sales support

## Compensation & Benefits

- **Job Type:** Full Time
- **Experience Required:** 5 years
- **Location:** Nairobi, Kenya
- **Field:** Energy, Automotive, Sales
