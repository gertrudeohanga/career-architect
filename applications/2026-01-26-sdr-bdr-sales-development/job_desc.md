# SDR/BDR (Sales Development Representative) Position

## Role Overview
**Position**: SDR/BDR (Sales Development Representative)  
**Location**: Nairobi, Kenya  
**Contract**: Full time  
**Salary**: KES 64,000 – 65,000/Monthly  
**Education**: Bachelor's degree  
**Experience**: 0 months (entry-level)  
**Language**: English (Fluent to Native proficiency: 9.0-10.0)

## Primary Responsibilities

### Client Outreach & Engagement
- Conduct client outreach and engagement initiatives
- Focus on upselling services to existing clients
- Reconnect with clients who have received proposals but haven't responded
- Conduct cold calls to new prospects
- Generate qualified leads for the sales team

### CRM Management
- Manage CRM systems (mandatory requirement)
- Track client interactions and follow-up activities
- Maintain accurate and up-to-date client information
- Generate reports and insights from CRM data

## Required Skills & Experience

### **Mandatory Requirements**
- **CRM Experience**: Proven experience managing CRM systems (HubSpot preferred, Salesforce acceptable)
- **Sales Background**: Experience in sales, business development, or client outreach
- **Service Industry**: B2B or B2C sales experience, preferably in service-based industries

### **Language Proficiency**
- **English Level**: 9.0 – 10.0 (Fluent to Native proficiency)

### **Soft Skills**
- **Communication**: Friendly and professional phone demeanor
- **Interpersonal**: Strong client communication and interpersonal skills
- **Motivation**: Independent and self-motivated work ethic
- **Teamwork**: Team-oriented with clear communication
- **Organization**: Excellent time management and organizational skills
- **Attention to Detail**: Ability to prioritize tasks and pay attention to detail
