# EBRD - Assistant Banking (Resident Office)

## Company Overview

The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) is an international financial institution extending operations to sub-Saharan African countries. The Bank is opening a Resident Office (RO) in Nairobi, Kenya, and operates with values of Inclusiveness, Innovation, Trust, and Responsibility.

## Role Summary

The Assistant provides comprehensive high-quality secretarial and administrative support to staff from various teams in the Resident Office. This role involves collaboration with international teams and requires excellent organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work in a multicultural environment.

## Key Responsibilities

### Administrative Support

- Screen telephone calls, take messages, and provide professional front-of-house service
- Manage diaries of Bankers to assist with smooth office operations and stakeholder schedules
- Proofread and draft routine business correspondence for Bankers' signature
- Handle editing, photocopying, scanning, and binding documents
- Organize conference calls, video conferences, and internal/external meetings

### Travel & Logistics

- Arrange travel for business trips including visa arrangements
- Follow up on reimbursement processes
- Work with service providers (travel agencies, courier companies, etc.)

### Project Support

- Provide administrative support in project preparation, due diligence, and monitoring
- Update project databases and file project documents
- Prepare contract request forms
- Assist in organizing workshops and seminars
- Prepare presentations for internal and external meetings

### Office Operations

- Support visiting missions to the Resident Office
- Cover reception area as required
- Handle ad-hoc secretarial and administrative duties as needed
