# Senior Retail Operations Manager – Fashion Retail

## Role Context
Critical role to strengthen retail structure as business scales. Manages **Area / Cluster Managers** to drive execution of retail strategy. Link between Head of Retail and field operations.

## Main Accountabilities
1.  **Sales & Performance:** Monitor cluster/store KPIs (revenue, conversion, basket size, ASP, shrinkage). Partner with merchandising on stock availability.
2.  **Operational Leadership:** Manage Cluster Leads. Ensure compliance with SOPs and brand standards.
3.  **People Leadership:** Coach Cluster Leads. Build succession pipelines.
4.  **Customer & Brand Excellence:** Champion Customer Experience framework. Ensure consistent visual merchandising.
5.  **Operational Efficiency:** Manage payroll, shrinkage, inventory accuracy. Support new store openings.
6.  **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Liaison with HQ (HR, Finance, Product). Support ERP integration.

## Requirements
-   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Business or Retail Management. MBA is an added advantage.
-   **Experience:** **8+ years** in multi-store retail management. **3+ years in a senior role** overseeing multiple regions/clusters within **Fashion Retail**.
-   **Skills:** Strong commercial acumen, analytical skills, people leadership.
-   **Conditions:** Travel between HQ and stores (Nairobi, Out of Town, Cross-border). Weekend work required.
