# Branch Manager - Nairobi

## Job Purpose
To develop and manage the Branch portfolio and lead the branch to ensure achievement of **business objectives** (deposits, assets, cards) and implementation of **service culture** while adhering to **policies and guidelines**.

## Key Responsibilities
1.  **Sales & Performance:**
    -   Cascade sales targets to staff.
    -   Track review performance.
    -   Meet High Net Worth (HNW) customers.
    -   Control cost and profitability (P&L).
2.  **Customer Service:**
    -   Maintain high service levels in Cash Mgmt, Remittances, and Account Opening.
    -   Reduce waiting time.
3.  **Service Quality & Ops Control:**
    -   **Audit:** Ensure null/low risk audit rating.
    -   **Cash Protocol:** Maintain low risk of cash difference, monitor daily MIS, check security items.
    -   **complaints:** Monitor mystery shopping results.
4.  **Internal Process:**
    -   Lead branch projects.
    -   Maintain archives.
    -   Ensure 100% closing of mandates.
5.  **Training & Development:**
    -   Analyze training needs.
    -   Conduct regular staff meetings.
    -   Manage leave and performance appraisals.

## Requirements
-   **Education:** Higher Diploma / Bachelor's degree (Master's is an advantage).
-   **Knowledge:** General Banking, Sharia principles (Advantage).
-   **Experience:**
    -   **Minimum 4 Years** in a similar role.
    -   Experience in Nairobi/Mombasa CBD is a **MUST**.
-   **Competencies:** Analytical Thinking, Leadership, Sharia Foundations, Relationship Management.
