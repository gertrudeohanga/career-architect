# TLC Worldwide - Business Development Manager

## Company Overview

**Industry**: MarTech / Loyalty & Rewards / Customer Engagement
**Size**: ScaleUp (400+ employees, 15 global hubs)
**Mission**: Help the world's biggest brands drive customer acquisition, loyalty and engagement with emotionally engaging, experience-led rewards

**About TLC**: TLC Worldwide helps global brands drive customer acquisition, loyalty, and engagement through experience-led rewards. From global banks to high street retailers, their programs shift behavior, boost ROI, and build brand love—at a fraction of the cost of discounting. Backed by COSMOS (their all-in-one program platform), they deliver personalized campaigns at scale with live data, smart insights, and seamless customer journeys. 30 years in business with a global network of 100,000+ rewards.
