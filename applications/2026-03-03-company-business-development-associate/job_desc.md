# Company - Business Development Associate

## Company Overview

A company operating across multiple sectors including real estate, construction, and merchandising. The organization operates in fast-paced environments and requires business development professionals who can engage diverse communities and stakeholders. The company appears to be growth-oriented with focus on B2B sales and market expansion.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Associate is a self-driven, charismatic, and results-oriented role focused on identifying and pursuing new business opportunities across targeted sectors with emphasis on B2B sales. The position requires someone who thrives in relationship-building, understands market dynamics, and is comfortable operating in fast-paced environments across real estate, construction, and merchandising industries. This is a contract position based in Nairobi requiring hybrid work setup with field presence.

## Key Responsibilities

### Business Development & Sales
- Identify and pursue new business opportunities across targeted sectors (focus on B2B sales)
- Hit and surpass achieved set of company sales targets
- Develop and execute sales strategies to drive revenue growth
- Negotiate contracts and close deals effectively
- Conduct market research to identify emerging trends and opportunities

### Relationship Management
- Build and maintain strong relationships with clients, partners, and key stakeholders
- Engage diverse communities and stakeholders across different settings
- Represent the company at meetings, site visits, networking events, and industry engagements

### Presentations & Reporting
- Prepare and deliver compelling sales presentations and proposals
- Provide regular reports on sales performance and pipeline development

## Required Qualifications

### Experience
- 4–6 years of proven experience in sales (Required)
- Particularly within real estate, construction, or merchandising sectors (Preferred)
- Demonstrated experience working with diverse communities and varied market segments

### Skills & Competencies
- Strong interpersonal, negotiation, and communication skills
- Self-motivated with high level of initiative and accountability
- Ability to work independently in hybrid setup while maintaining strong team collaboration

### Other Requirements
- Valid driving license (Mandatory)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in B2B sales
- Track record of hitting and surpassing sales targets
- Experience across multiple sectors (real estate, construction, merchandising)
- Network of contacts in target industries

## Key Competencies

- **Charismatic and confident communicator**: Naturally charming, confident in engaging diverse audiences
- **Strategic thinker with strong commercial acumen**: Understanding market dynamics and business opportunities
- **Relationship-driven and customer-focused**: Building and maintaining strong stakeholder relationships
- **Adaptable and culturally aware**: Comfortable working with diverse communities
- **Goal-oriented with strong closing ability**: Results-oriented, able to close deals effectively

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Presentation Tools**: PowerPoint, Google Slides for sales presentations
