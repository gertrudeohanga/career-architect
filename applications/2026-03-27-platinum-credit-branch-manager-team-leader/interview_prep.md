**Q10: What's your approach to credit risk assessment?**

**A**: My approach to credit risk assessment is thorough but practical. I look at multiple factors: repayment capacity (income vs. obligations), repayment history (if existing client or references), character (through conversation and references), and collateral (for secured loans). At My-Credit, I assessed group lending risk by evaluating both individual members and group dynamics - strong groups with good leadership had better repayment rates. I also believe in the "know your customer" principle - taking time to understand their business, cash flows, and challenges helps predict repayment ability. I balance being thorough with being efficient - I don't want to lose good clients to slow processes, but I also won't compromise on quality assessment. Documentation is critical - proper records protect both the company and the client.

**SAR Framework**:
- Situation: Needed to assess credit risk for 50+ groups (1,000+ members) at My-Credit
- Action: Evaluated multiple factors (capacity, character, collateral, group dynamics), conducted thorough assessments, maintained proper documentation
- Result: Maintained portfolio quality, achieved 120% of growth targets
