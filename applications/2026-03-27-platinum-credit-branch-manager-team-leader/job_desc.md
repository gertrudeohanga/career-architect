# Platinum Credit Limited - Branch Manager / Team Leader (Asset Financing)

## Company Overview

Platinum Credit Limited is a credit-only Micro-Finance Company licensed by the Central Bank of Kenya as a Digital Credit provider. Founded in 2003, the company provides emergency financial solutions across Kenya with a vision to expand across Africa. Platinum Credit delivers cash within 24 hours by leveraging technology and world-class customer experience. The company offers various financial products including Civil Servant Loans, Log Book Loans, Personal Loans, Asset Finance, Import Duty Finance, and Landlord Loans.

## Role Summary

Branch Manager or Team Leader position in the Asset Financing Division. The role focuses on leadership, sales performance, and delivering results in asset financing operations. This is an opportunity for driven, passionate professionals to lead teams and grow their careers in microfinance.

## Key Responsibilities

Based on typical Branch Manager/Team Leader roles in microfinance asset financing:

- Lead and manage branch/team operations in asset financing division
- Drive sales performance and achieve revenue targets
- Manage asset financing portfolio (log book loans, asset-backed lending)
- Lead, coach, and develop team members to achieve targets
- Ensure excellent customer experience and service delivery
- Implement risk management and credit assessment processes
- Monitor portfolio quality and manage delinquency
- Build and maintain relationships with clients and stakeholders
- Ensure compliance with CBK regulations and company policies
- Prepare and present performance reports to management
