# ICEA Lion Group - Corporate Business Development Officer (Coast Region & Mount Kenya)

## Company Overview

ICEA LION Group is a one-stop financial services provider offering innovative products and services in insurance, pensions, investments and trusts. The group was formed as a result of business reorganization involving Lion of Kenya Insurance Company Limited (LOK) and Insurance Company of East Africa (ICEA) in 2012. The group is a leading financial services provider in Kenya with a strong market presence.

## Role Summary

The Corporate Business Development Officer is responsible for promoting and growing the Group Life & Pensions business by providing competitive quotations to clients, generating revenue through diverse distribution channels, new business processing, and providing excellent customer service. The role covers Coast Region and Mount Kenya territories.

## Key Responsibilities

- Generate revenue from new and existing brokers as well as clients
- Provide and send competitive quotations to prospects and intermediaries
- Prepare and write proposals for submission and tender processing
- Consult on the most effective cover for a particular need, while taking a number of factors into account
- Set up meetings, prepare and deliver presentations to potential clients
- Process documentation for new business acquisition
- Develop and maintain good working relationships with intermediaries, business partners and existing customers
- Ensure credibility with clients by maintaining detailed knowledge of current market conditions and competitors' products
- Deliver good customer service by responding swiftly to queries and concerns from clients
- Gather customer service feedback and report on intellectual and operational issues raised by clients
- Handle compliance and servicing meetings as assigned by Managers
- Provide management with market feedback and intelligence
- Regularly and accurately update database of all prospective and closed business in addition to submitting weekly reports of activities to managers
- Ensure strict adherence to practices, procedures and policy stipulated in the business development operational manual including the Code of Ethics
- Maintain and update all forms of business contacts
- Prepare regular management reports
- Manage intermediaries and other business partners social forums to enhance relationships and reward good performance
- Manage exhibition stands and corporate sponsorships
- Provide advice to process improvement and system development initiatives affecting the department and participate in automation of business function
- Identify broker or emerging customer needs in order to help develop new business opportunities in liaison with business development heads

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's Degree in a business or social science related field from a reputable university
- At least four (4) years' post qualification experience in a Life Assurance and Pensions Business Development function
- Must have previous experience in sales and marketing
- Appreciable understanding of the Kenya insurance market, the prevailing business environment and products by the competition
- Good customer relationship management skills (internal and external customers)
- Good communications skills, both written and verbal
- Self-motivated but able to work as part of a team
- Good organizational and time-management skills
- Positive attitude, self-motivated, self-driven and able to work with minimal supervision
- Good negotiation and persuasion skills
- Confidence presenting to large groups of people
- Trustworthiness and discretion when handling confidential information
- A smart appearance and professional manner
- Strong attention to detail, ability to multitask and performance oriented

## Preferred Qualifications

- Reasonable progress toward professional qualification(s) in insurance e.g. ACII, FCII or equivalent, would be an added advantage

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **CRM Systems**: Database management for prospective and closed business
- **Microsoft Office**: Excel, PowerPoint, Word for reports and presentations
- **Proposal Tools**: Quotation and tender preparation systems
- **Other**: Insurance management systems, reporting tools
