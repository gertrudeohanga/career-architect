### Q3: Walk me through how you would prepare a competitive quotation for a corporate client seeking Group Life cover.

**Why they ask**: Testing quotation preparation process and consultative approach

**Your answer**:
"My approach would be:

1. **Needs Discovery**: Meet with client to understand: number of employees, age demographics, salary bands, existing cover, budget, specific requirements (death benefit multiples, disability cover, etc.)
2. **Risk Assessment**: Gather information on employee demographics, industry risk factors, claims history if switching insurers
3. **Product Positioning**: Recommend appropriate ICEA Lion Group Life products based on client needs and budget
4. **Competitive Pricing**: Work with underwriting team to develop competitive premium rates considering client risk profile and market conditions
5. **Proposal Development**: Prepare comprehensive quotation including: cover details, premium breakdown, benefits summary, claims process, service commitments, value-adds
6. **Presentation**: Present quotation to client, explain benefits, address questions, demonstrate ICEA Lion's competitive advantages
7. **Negotiation**: Handle objections, adjust cover or pricing if needed within guidelines, close the deal
8. **Follow-Up**: Maintain communication, provide additional information, move to documentation and onboarding

At Tenakata and Ngao Credit, I prepared loan quotations and proposals using similar consultative approach—understanding client needs, assessing risk, positioning appropriate products, presenting competitively. I would apply the same systematic methodology to insurance quotations."
