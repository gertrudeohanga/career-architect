# Direct Sales Executive

## Company Overview
I&M Bank is one of Kenya's leading commercial banks offering a full range of banking and financial services to retail and corporate customers.

## Purpose of the Role
Reporting to the Team-leader Sales, the incumbent is responsible for selling the bank's products, identifying and following up on leads, building relationships, and interacting directly with customers throughout the sales process.

## Key Responsibilities

- Marketing and selling bank products and services to potential customers
- Contacting leads and setting up appointments for product presentations
- Assisting clients in completing forms and submitting them for processing
- Attending activations, door-to-door marketing, and other organized promotional activities
- Compiling regular sales reports (daily, weekly, monthly quotas)
- Efficiently following up on leads and maintaining consistent communication to ensure customer satisfaction
- Cross-selling to existing clientele
- Delivering excellent customer experience to ensure sustainable organic growth

## Required Qualifications

- Diploma or Degree in a business-related field

## Required Experience

- Relevant work experience as a Sales Representative with a proven track record

## Skills Required

- Highly motivated and target-driven
- Excellent selling, negotiation, and communication skills
- Strong prioritizing, time management, and organizational skills
- Ability to create and deliver tailored presentations
- Relationship management skills and openness to feedback
