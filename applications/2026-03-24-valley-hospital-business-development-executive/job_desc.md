# Valley Hospital - Business Development Executive

## Company Overview

Valley Hospital is a leading Level 4 hospital in Nakuru City offering both inpatient and outpatient services. The hospital is focused on expanding its outreach and growth across targeted neighbouring counties through strategic business development initiatives.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Executive will be responsible for identifying new business opportunities, building and managing stakeholder relationships, and promoting hospital services. This role is central to enhancing brand visibility, growing patient referrals, and strengthening the hospital's presence through community and institutional engagement across assigned counties.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identify new business opportunities for hospital services expansion
- Build and manage stakeholder relationships with corporates, insurance providers, and community organizations
- Promote hospital services to increase patient referrals and brand visibility
- Strengthen hospital presence through community and institutional engagement
- Plan and execute community engagements, Continuing Medical Education (CME) sessions, and medical camps
- Conduct market analysis and competitor research
- Prepare business reports and strategic recommendations
- Network extensively within assigned counties to build hospital reputation
- Implement strategic outreach initiatives based on market trends
- Manage relationships with insurance and corporate health schemes
- Travel extensively within assigned counties for business development activities

## Required Qualifications

- Diploma in Marketing, Business, or a related field
- Minimum 3 years of experience in sales, marketing, or business development
- Preferably experience in the healthcare sector
- Good understanding of insurance and corporate health schemes
- Willingness to travel extensively within assigned counties

## Preferred Qualifications

- Healthcare sector experience
- Established network in Nakuru and neighbouring counties
- Experience with medical camps and CME organization
- Understanding of hospital services and patient referral systems

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Business Tools**: CRM systems, Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
- **Marketing**: Community engagement platforms, event coordination tools
- **Analysis**: Market research tools, competitor analysis frameworks
- **Other**: Reporting systems, presentation tools
