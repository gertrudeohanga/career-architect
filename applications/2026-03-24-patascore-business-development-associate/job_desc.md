# Patascore (Pezesha) - Business Development Associate

## Company Overview

Patascore is a flagship B2B business line of Pezesha, preparing to operate as an independent entity from 2026. The company delivers cutting-edge credit infrastructure solutions to financial and non-financial institutions across Kenya and the region. Pezesha, founded in late 2016, is a digital financial infrastructure platform connecting SMEs to working capital through collaborative approaches with banks, MFIs, and other financial institutions, driving meaningful financial inclusion.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Associate will drive topline revenue growth through positioning and selling Patascore products. This role combines sales execution, digital marketing, events coordination, and market research to translate commercial activities into sustainable revenue growth. The position is instrumental in positioning Patascore as a key revenue driver in 2026, building on strong momentum achieved in 2025 with minimal marketing investment.

## Key Responsibilities

- Drive lead generation, partner acquisition, and onboarding across target markets
- Execute quarterly commercial strategies and consistently meet or exceed sales targets
- Own the full sales lifecycle: prospecting, pitching, negotiation, onboarding, integration, and post-launch tracking
- Build and manage a strong, data-driven sales pipeline using CRM tools
- Develop and maintain strong relationships with key decision-makers (Risk Officers, Product Heads)
- Drive retention and revenue growth within existing accounts through upselling and optimization
- Ensure high partner satisfaction, product adoption, and long-term engagement
- Build deep expertise across all Patascore products to support effective positioning and cross-selling
- Lead and support product demos, client presentations, and stakeholder engagements
- Represent Patascore at industry events, forums, and networking opportunities
- Conduct market research and competitor analysis to inform strategy, pricing, and product development
- Collaborate closely with Product, Data Science, Operations, and Marketing teams to deliver tailored partner solutions
- Provide structured feedback from clients to inform product roadmap and enhancements

## Required Qualifications

- Minimum 3 years' experience in Business Development, Sales, or Partnerships within fintech, credit, or financial services
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Economics, or a related field
- Strong B2B sales, negotiation, and closing skills
- Excellent communication and presentation skills (including executive-level engagement)
- Strong analytical mindset with attention to detail
- Proficiency in CRM tools and Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Technical aptitude: Understanding of credit risk, data-driven decisioning tools, and SaaS/LaaS models
- Domain knowledge: Familiarity with digital lending, merchant financing, and credit scoring ecosystems
- Relationship management: Ability to build and sustain relationships with senior stakeholders
- Execution focus: Highly proactive, results-driven, and comfortable working in a fast-paced environment

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **CRM Tools:** Sales pipeline management systems
- **Microsoft Office:** Excel (data analysis), PowerPoint (presentations)
- **Products:** Alternative credit scoring, Elimiza, Loan Management as a Service (LMS), Decisioning and underwriting solutions
- **Other:** Digital marketing tools, event coordination platforms
