# Primano Global Limited - Business Development Officer

## Company Overview

Primano Global Limited is a fast-growing technology company operating in the fintech/retail-tech space. The company's retail vertical operates under the brand name "Primano RetailX" and provides Point of Sale (POS), Inventory Management software, and Online store solutions to retail and hospitality businesses across Kenya. The company focuses on digitizing operations for Retail stores, Restaurants, Bakeries, Meat Shops, Supermarkets, and Distributors.

## Role Summary

Business Development Officer responsible for driving sales of POS, Inventory software, and Online store solutions to retail and hospitality businesses across Kenya. This role combines B2B sales, business development, marketing support, and customer relationship management. The position requires field sales across Nairobi and other regions in Kenya, with a focus on consultative selling and complete sales cycle management from lead generation to deal closure.

## Key Responsibilities

**Sales & Business Development**
- Drive sales of POS, Inventory software, and Online store solutions to Retail, Restaurants, Bakeries, Meat Shops, Supermarkets, and Distributors
- Identify, prospect, and onboard new customers through field sales, referrals, and channel partnerships
- Conduct product demos and presentations tailored to customer business needs
- Manage the complete sales cycle from lead generation to deal closure
- Meet or exceed monthly and quarterly sales targets

**Marketing & Market Expansion**
- Support local marketing initiatives to increase product visibility and lead generation
- Gather market intelligence on competitors, pricing, and customer requirements
- Participate in exhibitions, roadshows, and partner events when required
- Collaborate with the marketing team to develop sales collateral and campaigns

**Customer Engagement & Retention**
- Build and maintain strong relationships with key decision-makers
- Work closely with implementation and support teams to ensure smooth onboarding
- Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities within existing accounts
- Ensure high customer satisfaction and long-term retention

## Required Qualifications

- Minimum 2+ years of experience in selling POS, Inventory, ERP, or Retail/Restaurant solutions
- Proven track record of achieving sales targets in a B2B environment
- Strong understanding of retail and hospitality business workflows
- Diploma or bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, IT, or a related field
- Experience in field sales across Nairobi and other regions in Kenya is an advantage

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with software/SaaS sales
- Existing network in retail or hospitality sectors
- Knowledge of fintech/retail-tech solutions
- Experience with channel partnerships and referral programs

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Software Solutions:** POS systems, Inventory Management software, ERP systems, Online store platforms
- **Sales Tools:** CRM tools, MS Office (required proficiency)
- **Technical:** Basic technical understanding of software solutions and integrations
