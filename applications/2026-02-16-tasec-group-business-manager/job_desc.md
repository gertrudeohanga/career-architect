# Business Manager

## Job Summary
Oversee operations, drive revenue growth, and support strategic objectives across multiple business sectors (Education, Consulting, Real Estate, Tech). Focus on performance, efficiency, team coordination, and client management.

## Key Responsibilities
-   **Revenue:** Drive business growth, revenue performance, and profitability.
-   **Strategy:** Develop and implement business strategies/ops plans.
-   **Financials:** Monitor budgets, forecasts, and KPIs.
-   **Operations:** Oversee day-to-day operations and cross-functional coordination.
-   **Clients:** Manage client relationships, partnerships, and contract negotiations.
-   **Growth:** Identify new business opportunities and process improvements.
-   **Compliance:** Ensure regulatory compliance.

## Qualifications
-   **Education:** Bachelor's degree.
-   **Experience:** 5 years.
-   **Skills:** Strategy-led, Data-informed, Commercial acumen.

## About Company
TASEC Group is a diversified business group (Education, Consulting, Real Estate, Tech).
