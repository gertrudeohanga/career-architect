## Key Responsibilities

### **Data Management & Integration**
- Design and maintain a single data source for Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system
- Manage Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform data integration
- Oversee supply chain portals and sales route plan systems data
- Maintain HR records data integrity and consistency
- Systematically collect and analyze external data to supplement internal data

### **Data Engineering & Pipeline Development**
- Collaborate with IT to develop automated data pipelines
- Implement validation rules and maintain documentation for all data sources
- Ensure data transformation processes are properly documented and maintained
- Monitor data quality and integrity across all systems

### **Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics**
- Use demand forecasts to calculate optimal nomination volumes for OTS imports
- Monitor daily available storage space at KPC and private depots
- Issue evacuation alerts to logistics and sales teams when capacity is reached
- Track supply chain efficiency and operational bottlenecks

### **Financial Analytics & Reporting**
- Produce weekly reports on non-compliant customers
- Analyze days products stay in pipeline per customer
- Monitor payment timelines versus order booking
- Track cancelled or edited orders and evacuation efficiency
- Cover Kenya, Uganda and private depots operations

### **Market Intelligence & Competitive Analysis**
- Track Platts indices and import trends
- Monitor competitor pricing in collaboration with costing team
- Analyze market trends and competitive positioning
- Provide insights for strategic pricing decisions

### **Financial Modeling & Risk Management**
- Build dynamic models to forecast daily cash requirements
- Calculate tax and duty payment forecasts
- Project OTS settlements and operational expenses
- Monitor foreign-exposure and assess profit-and-loss impact
- Maintain risk register for supply, logistics, financial and regulatory risks

### **Dashboard Development & Visualization**
- Develop interactive dashboards in Power BI, Tableau or similar tools
- Provide real-time visibility into liquidity and sales performance
- Monitor stock days cover and depot congestion metrics
- Track operational efficiency KPIs and performance indicators

### **Strategic Reporting & Decision Support**
- Prepare comprehensive monthly business review packs
- Develop investment proposals and strategic decision summaries
- Support executive decision-making with data-driven insights
- Create presentations for senior management and stakeholders
