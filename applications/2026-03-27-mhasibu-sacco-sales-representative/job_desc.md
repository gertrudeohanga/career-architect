# Mhasibu Sacco - Sales Representative

## Company Overview

Mhasibu Sacco is a Savings and Credit Cooperative Society (SACCO) operating in Kenya. SACCOs are member-owned financial cooperatives that provide savings and credit services to their members.

## Role Summary

Sales Representative position based in Nairobi focused on member recruitment, portfolio growth, and promoting SACCO products and services to existing and prospective customers.

## Key Responsibilities

- Achieve monthly sales targets arising from member recruitment and increasing the Society's portfolio
- Present, promote and sell products/services using solid arguments to existing and prospective customers
- Maintain effective customer service to meet clients' expectations
- Achieve timely development of sales proposals, presentations, and reports
- Coordinate sales effort with team members and other departments
- Collect feedback from members at various networking forums for business leads and product/service improvement
- Participate in marketing activities and events
- Analyse territory/market potential, track sales and status reports
- Any other lawful duties that may be assigned from time to time

## Required Qualifications

**Education**:
- Diploma in Business related field or equivalent from a recognized university
- Relevant degree and membership with relevant professional body is an added advantage

**Experience**:
- Minimum 1 year relevant experience in a similar position


## Preferred Qualifications

- Degree in Business or related field
- Professional membership (e.g., Marketing Society of Kenya, Institute of Certified Public Accountants)
- Experience in SACCO or financial services sector

## Key Competencies

- Sales and business development
- Customer service excellence
- Member recruitment and retention
- Product knowledge and presentation
- Territory/market analysis
- Report writing and presentation
- Team coordination
- Networking and relationship building
