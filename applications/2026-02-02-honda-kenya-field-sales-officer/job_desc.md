# Field Sales Officer

## Company Overview
A prominent distributor of motorcycles in Kenya with a strong presence in the market (Honda Motorcycle Kenya). The company works with authorized Honda dealers across the country.

## Role Summary
The main scope of this position is to expand motorcycle sales to the dealers (Wholesales) and from the dealers to the customers (Retail Sales).

## Key Relationships

**Internal:**
- Sales department
- Service department
- Parts department

**External:**
- Authorized Honda dealers
- Motorcycle customers (existing and potential)
- Roadside mechanics and Honda service points
- Corporate Clients

## Key Responsibilities

- Support dealers in the region assigned through various approved actions to increase their motorcycle sales
- Monitor dealer stock movement and negotiate for Wholesale sales from HMK
- Corporate sales
- Support dealer development by assisting in scouting of suitable new 3S dealers
- Gathering of marketing intelligence in the region assigned
- Issuing regular reports as directed by superior
- Sourcing and closing on corporate deals
- Expanding the market share for Honda motorcycle Kenya
- Proposing and implementing marketing strategies
- Performing any other roles as directed by superior

## Required Qualifications

- University Degree/Diploma (added advantage)
- Proficient in computers

## Required Experience

- **3-5 years** experience in a similar position or sales in motorcycles
- Corporate sales experience (added advantage)
- Good understanding of sales and marketing, especially in motorcycle industry
- Requires minimum supervision
- Able to meet deadlines
- Willing to work anywhere in the country

## Salary

KShs 60,000 - 90,000 (Gross)
