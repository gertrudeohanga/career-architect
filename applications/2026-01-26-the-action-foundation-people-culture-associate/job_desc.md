# The Action Foundation - People & Culture Associate

## Organization Overview
**The Action Foundation (TAF)** - Non-profit organization dedicated to improving quality of life for Children and Youth with Disabilities (CYWDs) through health and inclusive education services since 2009.

## Mission & Impact
- **Mission**: Champion disability-inclusive service delivery and create pathways for CYWDs to reach their full potential
- **Impact**: Delivered services to over 20,000 children and youth with disabilities
- **Approach**: Lifecycle programs from early years to adulthood through outreach centers and partnerships

### 1. Talent Acquisition & Onboarding
- Support end-to-end recruitment processes (job postings, shortlisting, interview coordination, reference checks)
- Coordinate employee onboarding and induction for smooth integration

### 2. Employee Lifecycle Management

### 3. Performance Management & Learning
- Coordinate performance appraisal processes and documentation

### 4. Employee Relations & Staff Well-being
- Serve as first point of contact for HR-related queries

### 5. HR Policies, Compliance & Safeguarding

- Support disciplinary and grievance processes with due process

### 6. Data, Reporting & Documentation
- Maintain HR data and generate routine HR reports for management
- Support HR audits, compliance checks, and documentation requirements
- Ensure confidentiality and data protection of employee information
- Maintain accurate and organized HR records

### 7. Organizational Culture & Inclusion
- Strengthen organizational culture, inclusion, and diversity initiatives
- Promote disability-inclusive workplace practices
- Support internal communication and culture-building initiatives
- Foster positive and inclusive work environment
