# Kaziweza Limited - Branch Team Leader - Eldoret

## Company Overview

Kaziweza Limited is a furniture and home décor retail company operating in Kenya. The company runs showroom-based operations with a focus on customer service, sales performance, and operational excellence. Kaziweza operates multiple branches across Kenya with emphasis on inventory management, financial controls, and team development.

## Role Summary

The Branch Team Leader oversees all aspects of branch operations including showroom sales, stock and inventory control, cash management, and staff supervision. This senior-level position ensures accurate financial reporting, effective team collaboration, compliance with company policies, and achievement of branch targets through sales performance, optimal stock levels, excellent customer service, and team development.

## Key Responsibilities

### Branch Operations & Administration
- Supervise all branch activities including sales, stock control, customer service, and staff performance
- Ensure smooth daily running of the showroom while upholding company standards and policies
- Manage staff schedules, assign duties, and monitor performance
- Ensure necessary documents are filled by design consultants per standard procedures
- Verify and maintain leave records, conduct payroll sheet verifications
- Ensure data is correctly entered in the system with accurate documentation
- Ensure all accounts needing cancellation are attended within the same week
- Run Outstanding Booking reports for system housekeeping
- Follow up on reports printed, distributed, reviewed, and worked out per schedule

### Financial, Cash Handling & Compliance
- Oversee cash desk operations ensuring all procedures are fully respected
- Undertake continuous monitoring and management of debtors
- Review unpaid accounts weekly and ensure arrears procedures are followed
- Assist in formulation of budgets and cash flows for the branch
- Limit unnecessary expenses including petty cash
- Ensure compliance with internal controls and financial policies

### Stock & Inventory Management
- Oversee stock levels to ensure availability of fast-moving furniture and décor items
- Coordinate with inventory, warehouse, and supply chain teams on stock replenishment
- Conduct regular stock counts and reconciliations to maintain accurate inventory records
- Investigate and resolve stock discrepancies
- Oversee security aspects and prevention of theft and fraud
- Verify product codes from system match POS tickets on shop floor
- Ensure items in showroom are displayed according to range plan with correct prices

### Sales & Customer Service
- Inspire sales team to achieve targets through customer-focused strategies
- Ensure branch achieves set targets
- Give excellent service to customers
- Deal with customer complaints tactfully
- Ensure marketing tools are used optimally and promotional information is communicated
- Oversee proper displaying of products in showroom
- Undertake activities with sales team to boost sales
- Follow up on outstanding Delivery Notes and Bookings from Design Consultants
- Assist in identifying opportunities for new products and enhancement of existing products
- Monitor and report on competitor activities and identify business opportunities
- Understand the market and how company products are used
- Identify new markets (geographical and industry sector)

### Staff Management & Development
- Lead, mentor, and coach branch staff to achieve sales and operational goals
- Conduct performance appraisals and recommend training
- Design and conduct regular team trainings on sales, customer service, stock handling, market trends, and policies
- Ensure effective internal communications across teams
- Communicate marketing and promotional information to walk-in clients and staff
- Plan staff leave schedule and approve staff leave
- Foster culture of energy, people centricity, integrity, and customer centricity

## Required Qualifications

### Education & Certification
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Commerce, Supply Chain, or related field (Required)
- CPA II qualification (Added advantage)

### Experience
- 3-5 years' experience in retail branch management or supervision (Required)
- Experience in furniture or home décor industry (Added advantage)
- Proven experience in showroom sales management and customer engagement
- Strong knowledge of stock and inventory management

### Technical Skills
- Proficiency in ERP/stock management systems
- MS Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
- Experience with Odoo (Desirable)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Furniture or home décor industry experience
- Odoo ERP system experience
