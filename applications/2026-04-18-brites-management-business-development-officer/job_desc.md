## Company Overview

Brites Management is a recruitment and staffing agency placing candidates in various corporate roles across Kenya.

---

## Role Summary

Business Development Officer responsible for client relationship management, business development, sales, market research, and performance tracking. Focus on building corporate client relationships and driving business growth.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Client Relationship Management
- Build and maintain long-term relationships with corporate clients
- Serve as the main point of contact for client inquiries and requests
- Understand client needs and provide tailored solutions to strengthen partnerships

### Business Development & Sales
- Identify potential business opportunities and leads
- Develop strategies to acquire new clients and expand market reach
- Negotiate and close business deals to achieve sales targets
- Collaborate with internal teams to ensure smooth service delivery and client satisfaction

### Market Research & Insights
- Stay up to date on current market trends, competitor activities, and customer needs
- Provide actionable insights and recommendations to management for strategic decision-making
- Monitor industry developments to identify growth opportunities

### Reporting & Performance Tracking
- Maintain accurate records of sales activities, client interactions, and deals closed
- Prepare regular reports on performance, market trends, and business development progress

---

## Required Qualifications

### Education
- Degree in Marketing, Business, or related field

### Experience
- Minimum 2 years of experience in corporate sales or business development

### Skills & Competencies
- Excellent negotiation, communication, and closing skills
- Strong interpersonal skills with ability to build trust and credibility
- Results-oriented, proactive, and self-motivated
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple clients simultaneously
- Strong analytical skills to evaluate market trends and client needs
- Professional and confident in corporate settings
- Driven to achieve targets and contribute to company growth
