# Studio Twenty Two Agencies (ABELA) LIMITED - Business Development Manager

## Company Overview

Studio Twenty Two Agencies (ABELA) LIMITED is a recruitment and staffing agency operating in Kenya. The company specializes in connecting qualified professionals with organizations across various sectors including Admin & Office roles.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Manager will be responsible for driving business growth through identifying new opportunities, building client relationships, and developing strategic initiatives to increase revenue and market presence. This is a senior-level position requiring 5 years of experience in business development or sales.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identifying and pursuing new business opportunities
- Building and maintaining strong client relationships
- Developing strategies to increase revenue and market presence
- Preparing business proposals, presentations, and reports
- Collaborating with cross-functional teams to achieve organizational goals
- Monitoring market trends and competitor activities

## Required Qualifications

- A degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or a related field
- 2–5 years of experience in business development or sales
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office and CRM software
- Strong communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- High level of professionalism and integrity
- Flexibility to travel when required
