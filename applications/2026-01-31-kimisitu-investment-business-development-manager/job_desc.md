# Business Development Manager

## Company Overview
Kimisitu Investment Company Limited (KICL) is an investment company focused on real estate and investment products, serving both individual and corporate clients through direct sales and strategic partnerships.

## Key Responsibilities

### Sales Strategy & Revenue Growth
- Develop and implement annual/medium-term strategies
- Drive revenue targets and market share growth
- Identify new markets and revenue streams

### Sales Leadership & Execution
- Lead prospecting, negotiations, and closure for individual and corporate deals
- Manage agents and sales structures
- Ensure ethical selling practices

### Marketing & Brand Management
- Oversee product positioning, pricing, and promotional campaigns
- Manage digital marketing and corporate communications

### Partnerships
- Structure and manage strategic partnerships (banks, SACCOs, developers)
- Drive co-marketing and distribution initiatives

### Customer Experience
- Build strong client relationships
- Oversee onboarding, retention, and feedback management

### Team Leadership
- Lead, coach, and develop the sales and marketing team
- Set KPIs and conduct performance reviews

### Reporting & Compliance
- Prepare reports for Management and the Board
- Manage budgets
- Ensure compliance with CMA regulations and internal policies

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Marketing, Commerce, Economics, Finance, or related field
- Master's/MBA is an added advantage
- Membership in a professional body (e.g., MSK, CIM) is an added advantage

## Required Experience

- 7–10 years in business development, sales, and marketing (preferably in real estate or investments)
- 3–5 years in a management or senior supervisory role
- Proven ability to drive revenue growth and manage high-performance teams

## Skills Required

- Commercial and strategic acumen
- Strong sales leadership and people management
- Negotiation and relationship management
- Marketing and brand management capability
- Analytical ability and data-driven decision-making
- High ethical standards and governance awareness
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
