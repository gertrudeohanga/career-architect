# SimplePay Capital Limited - Business Development Officer

## Company Overview

SimplePay Capital Limited is a financial services company operating in Kenya's lending sector, providing loan products to customers across the country. The company focuses on collections, customer service, and portfolio management with a branch-based operational model.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Officer is responsible for achieving branch-level targets across disbursements, collections, and customer acquisition while ensuring adherence to standard operating procedures. This is a field-based role focused on customer payments collection, account management, and customer service delivery in the Coastal Region.

## Key Responsibilities

### Branch Targets
- Achieve monthly disbursement targets
- Meet collections targets and payment due dates
- Acquire new customers according to targets
- Maintain and grow active customer base
- Ensure adherence to all Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

### Collections & Account Management
- Review open accounts for collection efforts
- Collect customer payments in accordance with payment due dates
- Monitor payment schedules and follow up on overdue accounts

### Customer Service & Compliance
- Deliver excellent customer service
- Conduct Know Your Customer (KYC) and due diligence procedures
- Serve as information desk for customer inquiries
- Handle administration duties

## Required Qualifications

- **Education**: Diploma (minimum qualification)
- **Experience**: 2 years minimum experience (Mid-level)
- **Location**: Based in Coastal Region, Kenya
- **Employment Type**: Full-time

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in financial services, microfinance, or lending sector
- Strong collections and customer relationship management skills
- Knowledge of KYC and regulatory compliance requirements
- Field-based sales and customer acquisition experience
- Ability to work independently and meet targets

## Tech Stack / Tools

- Loan Management Systems
- CRM platforms
- Customer database management
- Mobile payment platforms (likely M-Pesa integration)
- Reporting and compliance systems

- **Benefits**: Not specified
