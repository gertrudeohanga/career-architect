# QONA SACCO - Marketing Assistant

## Company Overview

QONA SACCO is a member-focused financial cooperative committed to empowering its members through accessible, innovative, and sustainable financial solutions. The organization is currently in a growth phase, expanding its member base and product offerings.

## Role Summary

A results-driven and proactive Marketing Assistant to support member recruitment, product sales, and brand visibility as part of the SACCO's growth strategy.

## Key Responsibilities

- Recruit and retain new individual and corporate members, ensuring they remain active
- Cross-sell Sacco products to meet set sales targets
- Drive loan penetration and ensure achievement of set objectives
- Identify prospective leads, schedule meetings, follow up on presentations, and close sales deals
- Build and maintain relationships with referral sources, both internally and externally
- Provide regular feedback to management on market acceptance and recommend improvements
- Enhance the visibility and brand image of QONA SACCO

## Required Qualifications

- **Education:** Diploma in Sales & Marketing, Co-operative Management, or a business-related field
- **Experience:** Minimum of 2 years in a marketing role, preferably in a cooperative or financial institution

## Preferred Qualifications

- Customer care training
- Experience in the cooperative movement

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Systems:** SACCO management systems
- **Channels:** Member recruitment platforms, referral networks
- **Other:** CRM tools, presentation software

## Key Skills and Competencies

- Passionate about customer advocacy and engagement
- Strong understanding of changing market dynamics
- High level of professionalism and integrity
- Excellent communication, interpersonal, and negotiation skills

## Compensation & Benefits

- **Salary Range:** Not specified
- **Contract Type:** Full-time
