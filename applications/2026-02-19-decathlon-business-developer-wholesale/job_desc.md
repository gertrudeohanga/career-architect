# Decathlon - Business Developer Wholesale

## Company Overview

Decathlon is a global sports retail brand operating in the retail, fashion, and FMCG sector. The company focuses on making sports accessible to everyone through quality sporting goods and equipment. Decathlon is expanding its presence in East Africa through wholesale and distribution channels, targeting resellers, distributors, major companies, NGOs, and schools. The role is being recruited through BrighterMonday Consulting.

## Role Summary

Business Developer Wholesale responsible for driving market expansion and penetration in East African countries by converting market opportunities into long-term, profitable commercial partnerships. This role leads the entire acquisition cycle from prospecting and market analysis to onboarding and initial growth (3-6 months), ultimately accelerating business expansion and building scalable commercial foundations aligned with Decathlon's strategic goals. The position focuses on developing business with resellers, distributors, major companies, NGOs, and schools across East Africa.

## Key Responsibilities

**Develop and Acquire New Customers**
- Build a qualified pipeline of potential wholesale partners
- Conduct market mapping to identify opportunities
- Lead the end-to-end acquisition process to identify and engage potential partners (resellers, distributors, major companies, NGOs, schools)

**Build Sustainable Commercial Partnerships**
- Define commercial conditions and negotiate product ranges
- Create onboarding strategy for new partners
- Ensure smooth operational setup with internal teams (supply, finance, digital)
- Negotiate contracts, manage margins, and establish pricing structures

**Drive Growth of New Accounts (3-6 months)**
- Monitor sell-in/sell-through performance during ramp-up phase
- Identify quick wins to accelerate account growth
- Organize support including training and marketing for new partners
- Hand over mature accounts to Key Account Manager after initial growth phase

**Contribute to Business Channel Expansion Strategy**
- Share market data and competitive benchmarking
- Collaborate on building efficient onboarding and sales enablement processes
- Support overall channel development and scalability
- Analyze market potential and competitor activity
