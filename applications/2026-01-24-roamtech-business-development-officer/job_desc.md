# Roamtech Solutions Limited - Business Development Officer

## Company Overview

Roamtech Solutions Limited is an ICT/technology company focused on platform solutions and technology services. The company operates in a highly commercial environment with emphasis on B2B sales, reseller marketing, and strategic analysis.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Officer will be responsible for driving sales growth and achieving set revenue targets. The officer will identify and pursue new business opportunities, strengthen client relationships, enhance product visibility, and ensure consistent delivery of high-quality customer service to both existing and potential clients. This is an on-site, contract position in Nairobi, Kenya.

## Key Responsibilities

- Drive the development and growth of Key Accounts
- Interpret client needs and propose value-adding solutions that address client needs and objectives
- Liaise with relevant teams to ensure proposed client solutions are developed and monetized within company platforms
- Propose improvements of the user experience within company platforms
- Lead and develop implementation of approved product strategies to enhance revenue growth
- Oversee development of existing and new products and solutions
- Develop and implement sales and business development plans for Key Accounts and resellers
- Promote business growth by managing customer relations and ensuring timely resolution of customer complaints
- Provide feedback to management on insightful market intelligence and research
- Provide regular reporting on performance of direct sales and resellers
- Lead and ensure high performance, motivation and alignment within the team

## Key Measures of Performance

- Achievement of set revenue targets
- Customer acquisition
- Customer retention
- Customer delight through well-delivered user experience
- New business growth and development
- People management of team to ensure optimal delivery
- Supplier & vendor management
