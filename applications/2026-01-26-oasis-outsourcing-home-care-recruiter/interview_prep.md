# Interview Preparation Guide: Oasis Outsourcing Home Care Recruiter

## Company & Role Understanding

### Oasis Outsourcing Overview
- **Focus**: Human resources and recruitment outsourcing for home care services
- **Mission**: Connecting compassionate caregivers with clients needing quality home care
- **Values**: Meaningful work, collaborative environment, community impact

### Role Priorities
1. **Recruitment & Sourcing**: Find and attract qualified caregivers, nurses, and home health aides
2. **Interview & Assessment**: Evaluate candidates for qualifications and cultural fit
3. **Relationship Management**: Partner with hiring managers and maintain candidate relationships
4. **Compliance & Process**: Ensure adherence to employment laws and internal policies
5. **On-site Presence**: Full availability at Nairobi location

## Likely Interview Questions & Responses

### **Recruitment Experience**

**Q1: "Describe your experience with candidate assessment and interviewing."**

**Key Points:**
- Laterite Kenya: Conducted interviews with refugee populations assessing program effectiveness
- Maendeleo Ya Wanawake: Evaluated community members for program eligibility
- Ministry of Youth: Registered and screened youth groups for government programs
- Strong assessment and evaluation skills

**Sample Response:**
"I have extensive experience conducting interviews and assessing candidates across diverse populations. At Laterite Kenya, I conducted interviews with refugee populations to assess program effectiveness and individual needs, evaluating eligibility criteria for aid programs. At Maendeleo Ya Wanawake, I assessed community members for program participation and funding support. This experience has given me strong skills in evaluating qualifications, assessing cultural fit, and making informed decisions about candidate suitability."

**Q2: "How would you source candidates for home care positions?"**

**Key Points:**
- Experience with community engagement and networking
- Ability to use multiple platforms (job boards, social media, community platforms)
- Strong relationship building for referrals
- Understanding of diverse candidate populations

**Sample Response:**
"I would use a multi-channel approach to source home care candidates. I'd leverage job boards and social media platforms, but also focus on community engagement since I have experience building relationships across diverse communities. I would develop referral programs and attend community events to build networks. My experience working with various populations has taught me how to connect with different groups and understand their needs and motivations."
