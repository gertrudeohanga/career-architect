# Oasis Outsourcing - Home Care Recruiter (B2+ English)

## Company Overview
**Oasis Outsourcing** - Human resources organization providing recruitment and staffing solutions for home care services.

## Role Details
**Position**: Home Care Recruiter (On-Site)  
**Location**: Nairobi, Kenya  
**Contract**: Full time, On-site  
**Salary**: KES 85,000 - 100,000/Monthly  
**Education**: High school/secondary school  
**Experience**: 12 months minimum  
**Language**: English (B2+ level or higher)  
**Apply by**: February 1, 2026

## Key Responsibilities

### Recruitment & Sourcing
- Partner with hiring managers to understand staffing needs for home care services
- Create and publish job postings across job boards, social media, and community platforms
- Source candidates through networking, referrals, and recruitment events
- Maintain active talent pipelines and track recruitment metrics

### Interview & Selection
- Conduct initial interviews to evaluate qualifications, experience, and cultural fit
- Coordinate interviews with hiring managers and provide timely candidate feedback
- Manage the offer process, including salary discussions and onboarding coordination

### Compliance & Administration
- Ensure compliance with employment laws and internal company policies
- Maintain accurate recruitment records and documentation
- Utilize Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and HR databases
