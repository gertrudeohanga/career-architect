# Bank - Bancassurance Sales Representative

## Company Overview

A banking institution in Kenya offering bancassurance services (insurance products through banking channels). The organization operates through a branch network and provides both retail banking and insurance products to customers. The company is committed to diversity and encourages applications from qualified candidates with disabilities.

## Role Summary

Reporting to the Bancassurance Regional Sales Manager, the Bancassurance Sales Representative will drive sales of insurance and retail products in assigned branches. The role focuses on day-to-day insurance business management, providing quick, seamless, timely, efficient, and responsive service to customers at the branch level. This is a customer-facing sales role requiring strong product knowledge and relationship management skills.

## Key Responsibilities

### Sales Performance
- Achieve sales targets (both Retail & Insurance) in accordance with provided budgets
- Deliver exceptional sales performance by identifying and meeting customer needs
- Sell and cross-sell General and Life insurance products
- Drive Life Insurance sales and ensure set sales budgets are met
- Monitor own sales performance on daily, weekly, and monthly basis
- Provide results to Sales Managers regularly

### Customer Retention & Service
- Retain customers within assigned portfolio with 85% retention ratio target
- Own customer queries and complaints around insurance products
- Resolve customer issues in timely manner
- Escalate unresolved queries to Line Manager or Head Office Bancassurance team
- Provide timely feedback to clients on claims

### Documentation & Compliance
- Ensure accuracy of each application and all customer documents
- Accountable for accuracy and quality of applications and supporting documents
- Ensure all claims are reported to Head Office Bancassurance team as guided
- Assist clients to attach relevant documentation and complete claim forms properly
- Ensure premiums are collected strictly as per credit control policy
- Ensure compliance to Group's policies in delivering day-to-day business expectations

### Reporting & Training
- Provide pipeline data as requested
- Conduct branch staff training on Bancassurance products

## Required Qualifications

### Academic Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree from university recognized by Commission of University Education (Required)
- Master's degree is an added advantage

### Professional Qualifications (Must have one of the following)
- Certificate of Proficiency in Insurance (COP)
- Executive Certificate of Proficiency in Insurance
- BCOM (Insurance and Risk Management option)
- COP Exemption Certificate

### Work Experience
- At least 1 year of experience in sales (Required)
- Strong knowledge of Insurance Products (Required)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Master's degree
- Experience in bancassurance or financial services sales
- Experience working in branch banking environment
- Track record of meeting/exceeding sales targets
