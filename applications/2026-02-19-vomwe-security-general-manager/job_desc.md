# Vomwe Security Limited - General Manager

## Company Overview

Vomwe Security Ltd, established in 2019, is a security guard services provider offering solutions to clients across various industries. The company provides manned guarding (static/patrols), night surveillance operations, access control, guard dogs, alarms and security products, and tracking services, focusing on advanced technology to effectively respond to client requests.

## Role Summary

General Manager responsible for providing overall strategic leadership, operational oversight, and financial management of the security firm. The role has accountability across all services including canine services, night surveillance, and manned guarding operations, ensuring service excellence, regulatory compliance, client satisfaction, and sustainable business growth. Reports to Managing Director. Based in Kitengela.

## Key Responsibilities

- Provide overall leadership and strategic direction for the security firm, overseeing canine services, night surveillance, and manned guarding operations
- Manage day-to-day operations, including deployment, supervision, and performance of security personnel and K9 units
- Ensure compliance with all relevant security regulations, licensing requirements, and labour laws
- Lead client engagement, contract management, and service delivery against agreed SLAs
- Oversee budgeting, cost control, procurement, and resource utilisation
- Lead recruitment, training, discipline, and performance management of staff

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Security Management, Criminology, Business Administration, Public Administration, or related field
- Recognized training or certification in private security management, risk management, or K9 operations
- **Proven experience managing canine security units, surveillance systems, and large-scale guarding and night security operations**
- **Minimum of 7 years' experience in the security industry, with at least 3 years in a senior management or leadership role**
- Demonstrated track record in operations management, client relations, and regulatory compliance within the private security sector

## Required Skills

- Strong leadership, decision-making, and crisis management skills
- Solid understanding of private security operations
- Financial and operational planning capability with a business mindset
- Excellent client management and communication skills
- High integrity, discretion, and regulatory awareness
- Self-driven and motivated
- Able to work with little management oversight
- Driving skills
