# Business Development Specialist II, ESARO

## Job Summary
Lead the production of high-quality **funding applications** for institutional donors (Government, Multilateral, Foundations). Provide specialized assistance in the BD cycle across East and Southern Africa (ESARO).

## Roles and Key Responsibilities
1.  **Proposal Leadership:** Lead positioning, capture planning, and proposal preparation.
2.  **Process Management:** Manage pre-positioning and proposal development to ensure responsiveness to donor requirements.
3.  **Proposal Coordinator:** Advise on opportunity analysis, go/no-go decisions, and coordinate partner inputs.
4.  **Writing:** Deploy as **Lead or Support Writer**, integrating technical/non-technical content.
5.  **Budgeting:** Deploy as budget lead or support.
6.  **Design Lead:** Guide technical strategy and structure design process.
7.  **Quality Assurance:** Strengthen design quality (Evidence-based, MEAL, Gender, Climate).
8.  **Strategy:** Liaise with senior management to develop resource mobilization strategies.
9.  **Capacity Building:** Train and mentor staff in BD cycle skills.

## Qualifications
-   **Education:** Bachelor's degree in International Development, Relations, or related (Master's preferred).
-   **Experience:**
    -   **Minimum 8 Years** of **International Development experience**.
    -   At least 3 years in a developing country.
    -   Demonstrated experience in **Competitive Proposals** (Grants and Contracts).
    -   Experience with donors like **World Bank, FCDO, EU, USG**.
-   **Skills:** Exceptional **Writing Skills** (English grammar/AP style), Negotiation, Strategic Thinking.
-   **Languages:** Fluency in English (French/Arabic/Portuguese is a plus).
