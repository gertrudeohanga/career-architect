# Sanara Program - Gender, Inclusion and Data Quality Audit

## Company Overview

The Sanara Program is a development initiative focused on supporting creative entrepreneurs, with particular emphasis on youth employment, enterprise development, and the creative industries. The program operates with a focus on gender equality, social inclusion, and supporting marginalized groups including young women, refugees, displaced persons, and persons with disabilities.

## Role Summary

This consultancy role involves conducting a comprehensive gender audit of the Sanara Program to assess how gender equality and social inclusion are currently addressed within the program. The consultant will identify strengths, gaps, and opportunities for improvement across program design, delivery mechanisms, financing approaches, partnerships, communication, monitoring, and organizational culture.

## Key Responsibilities

- Conduct desk review of key program documents (proposals, manuals, work plans, reports, M&E tools, training materials, communications)
- Apply gender and inclusion analysis frameworks to identify gaps and opportunities affecting young women, refugees, displaced persons (RDPs), and persons with disabilities (PWDs)
- Assess roles, resources, participation, and decision-making patterns across gender and social groups using structured analytical tools
- Employ gender mainstreaming principles to assess integration of gender equality into program design and implementation
- Develop tools and questionnaires based on frameworks to ensure consistency and comparative value
- Evaluate how program strategies address barriers faced by young women, women with disabilities, and refugee youth in accessing financing, skills, and employment
- Facilitate key informant interviews with program staff, implementing partners, creative entrepreneurs, and stakeholders
- Conduct focus group discussions with program participants (women, men, persons with disabilities, and refugees)
- Assess integration of gender equality, disability inclusion, and refugee inclusion across the program and three focus subsectors
- Analyze gender sensitivity of Sanara financing model, including access criteria, risk assessment, and disbursement mechanisms
- Review program's monitoring and evaluation framework, including use of sex-, age-, and disability-disaggregated data
- Develop Gender Audit Report with practical recommendations and Gender and Inclusion Action Plan

## Required Qualifications

- Advanced degree in Gender Studies, Development Studies, Sociology, or related field
- Minimum 5 years of experience conducting gender audits or gender analysis in development programs
- Strong understanding of gender equality frameworks, intersectionality, and social inclusion (especially related to disability and refugee contexts)
- Demonstrated expertise in youth employment, enterprise development, or the creative industries
- Proven ability to conduct participatory and rights-based assessments
- Excellent analytical, facilitation, and report writing skills
- Familiarity with Kenyan socio-cultural and policy context is an asset

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with creative industries and youth employment programs
- Knowledge of financing models and access barriers for marginalized groups
- Experience working with refugee populations and persons with disabilities
- Understanding of monitoring and evaluation frameworks with disaggregated data

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Frameworks:** Gender analysis frameworks, intersectionality analysis, social inclusion frameworks
- **Tools:** Survey design, questionnaire development, data analysis tools
- **Documentation:** Report writing, action plan development, presentation skills
