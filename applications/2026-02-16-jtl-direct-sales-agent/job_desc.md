# Direct Sales Agent

## Job Responsibilities
-   **Source Leads:** Source new business leads and service accounts.
-   **Field Sales:** Visit targeted outlets for existing or potential sales.
-   **Presentations:** Adjust content of sales presentations to the outlet type.
-   **Dealer Focus:** Study dealer volume for training and education.
-   **Reporting:** Submit daily call reports, weekly work plans, and monthly territory analysis.
-   **Market Intel:** Monitor competition (pricing, products, merchandising).
-   **Customer Service:** Resolve complaints and investigate problems.

## Qualifications
-   **Education:** Degree/Diploma in business-related field preferred.
-   **Experience:** At least **1 year** in sales, customer relations, or merchandising.
-   **Skills:** Highly motivated, target-driven, communication skills, proactive attitude.
