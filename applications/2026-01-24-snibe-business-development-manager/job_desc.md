# Snibe - Business Development Manager Kenya

## Company Overview

Snibe is a leading company in the In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) and medical device field, specializing in diagnostic products and medical devices for healthcare applications.

## Role Summary

Business Development Manager responsible for expanding Snibe's product presence in the Kenyan and East African market, managing distributor relationships, and driving sales growth through strategic partnerships and key account management.

## Key Responsibilities

- Expand assigned regional market with SNIBE products to potential customer groups to meet set targets
- Conduct product training and marketing campaigns to enhance brand awareness
- Manage key accounts and maintain relationships with KOLs (Key Opinion Leaders) to enhance academic influence of Snibe
- Manage and support assigned distributors to ensure alignment with company strategy and targets
- Accurately forecast sales and growth opportunities in local market using sales tools and applications

## Required Qualifications

- **Education**: Bachelor's degree or above, majoring in medical, bio-medical, human science or related disciplines preferred
- **Experience**: Minimum 3 years working experience in sales, sales experience in IVD or other Medical Device field preferred
- **Market Access**: Have experience accessing public and private markets
- **Regional Resources**: Have resources in East Africa countries (Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, and Burundi)
