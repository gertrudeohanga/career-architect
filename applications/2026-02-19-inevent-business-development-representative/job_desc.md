# Business Development Representative

## About InEvent

InEvent is a leader in the global event technology industry, providing event technology and AV software solutions.

## Role Overview

InEvent is looking for enthusiastic and results-driven Business Development Representatives to join their team. This role is ideal for motivated, customer-focused individuals who enjoy building relationships, learning innovative technology, and supporting a high-performing sales team.

## Key Responsibilities

- Support sales team members in identifying, qualifying, and nurturing potential customers
- Create and maintain sales and marketing assets, including demos, presentations, and visual materials used in customer engagements
- Monitor and identify opportunities to learn about market trends, customer needs, and the event technology landscape
- Learn and develop a strong understanding of InEvent's event technology and AV software solutions to effectively support demos and presentations
- Assist in the sales process by preparing presentation decks, demo environments, and supporting materials, as well as coordinating meetings when needed
- Assist in the sales process by preparing sales materials, scheduling meetings, and following up on leads
- Engage with prospective customers through multiple channels (phone, email, and other outreach methods) to gather information and build initial relationships
- Participate actively in training and development programs to enhance sales skills and product knowledge
- Provide basic product information and assist in responding to initial customer inquiries, primarily through prepared marketing and demo content
- Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of sales support activities and materials within CRM systems (e.g., Pipedrive, HubSpot)
- Work closely with sales to support team revenue goals through high-quality design and presentation support
- Monitor and identify opportunities to improve demos, presentations, and materials based on market trends, customer feedback, and the event technology landscape

## Qualifications & Requirements

- Strong interest in sales, design, technology, and customer engagement
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to support clear and compelling presentations
- Experience using CRM systems (e.g., Pipedrive, HubSpot, or similar) is a plus
- Positive attitude, strong work ethic, and a willingness to learn and grow in a sales-support and design-focused role
- Ability to work independently while also collaborating closely with the sales team
- Strong organizational skills with attention to detail, especially in presentation and demo preparation
- Computer proficiency and familiarity with common business and presentation software applications
- Fluency in English for professional and business communication

## Why Join InEvent?

- Be part of a fast-growing global event technology company
- Gain hands-on experience in sales within a dynamic and innovative industry
- Access continuous learning and professional development opportunities
- Collaborate with a supportive, international team driven by growth and excellence
