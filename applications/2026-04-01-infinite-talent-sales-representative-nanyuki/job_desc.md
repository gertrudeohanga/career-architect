# Infinite Talent Recruiters - Sales Representative (Nanyuki)

## Company Overview

Infinite Talent Recruiters is placing candidates for a fast-growing social impact project in Nanyuki. This organization combines vocational training with food product sales, creating sustainable community impact through education and entrepreneurship. The project operates at the intersection of social development and commercial enterprise.

## Role Summary

This is a dual-mandate sales role that combines student recruitment for vocational training programs with product sales of food items (bread, cakes, chapati, chips). The position is field-based and community-focused, requiring direct engagement with schools, churches, youth groups, and local markets. This role goes beyond traditional selling to create meaningful community impact while driving revenue growth.

## Key Responsibilities

### Student Recruitment
- Lead student recruitment efforts for vocational training programs
- Engage directly with schools, churches, youth groups, and community organizations
- Identify, nurture, and convert potential students into enrolled trainees
- Build relationships with educational institutions and community leaders
- Track enrollment conversions and meet recruitment KPIs

### Product Sales
- Drive daily sales of food products (bread, cakes, chapati, chips)
- Ensure consistent market penetration and revenue generation
- Onboard retailers, wholesalers, and institutional buyers
- Expand customer base across Nanyuki market
- Meet daily and monthly sales targets

### Digital Marketing & Lead Generation
- Leverage WhatsApp, Facebook, and TikTok for lead generation
- Build brand visibility through social media engagement
- Generate and qualify leads through digital channels
- Close deals via digital platforms

### Performance Tracking & Reporting
- Monitor conversion rates for both recruitment and sales
- Track performance against KPIs
- Provide regular reports on sales, recruitment, and market activity
- Support growth strategy with data-driven insights

## Required Qualifications

- Proven sales experience with track record of meeting/exceeding targets

- Self-motivated and target-driven personality
- Effective follow-up and persistence
- Strong communication skills (verbal and written)

- Knowledge of Nanyuki market and community networks
- Experience with B2B sales (retailers, wholesalers, institutions)

- Previous experience in social impact or NGO sector
