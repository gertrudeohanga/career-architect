# Director / Head of Sales - BYD

## Job Purpose
The **BYD Sales Manager** is responsible for the management of BYD EV Sales in Kenya. The objective is to establish the BYD brand in Kenya by identifying the right customers & markets.

## Main Responsibilities

**Commercial**
- Formulate and implement sales strategies to expand customer base.
- Lead a dynamic sales team tailored to the EV market.
- Accompany Sales Executives for support & coaching.
- Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasts.
- Coordinate credit management and minimize bad debts.
- **Resource Management:** Monitor utilization of resources/demo cars.

**Operational**
- Conduct business prospecting to ensure monthly order intake.
- Manage inventory and approve customer vehicle orders.
- Confirm price levels and discounts.

## Requirements

**Qualification**
- Bachelor's Degree in Business, Engineering, or relevant field.
- Diploma in Sales and Marketing.

**Experience**
- **8 years** relevant working experience in the **automotive industry**.
- **4 years** experience in a **sales management position**.
- Experience in electric vehicle (EV) sales is an added advantage.
- Successful track record in establishing a new Brand.

**Competencies**
- Knowledge of EVs and telematics.
- Knowledge of Government of Kenya & KMI regulations.
- Financial knowledge and Credit management.
- Strong leadership and strategic agility.
