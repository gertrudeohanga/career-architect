# Jobgether - Business Capture Manager

## Job Description

This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for a Business Capture Manager - REMOTE. In this pivotal role, you will spearhead the efforts of identifying and pursuing new business opportunities while maintaining robust relationships with existing clients. Your strategic initiatives will directly contribute to the growth and revenue targets set forth by the management team. By guiding the growth strategy and operational efficiency, you will play a vital part in positioning our partner for success in securing future contracts. Collaboration with leadership and cross-functional teams will be essential as you work to optimize processes and improve profit margins.

- Attend marketing and outreach events.
