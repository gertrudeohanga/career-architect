# Area Sales Manager Central

## Job Purpose
To lead field sales force in consistently achieving sales objectives, co-developing field sales force strategy with the National sales manager, lead sales force projects, building and maintaining strong customer relationships and ensuring efficient sales operations.

## Main Responsibilities
- **Sales Strategy Implementation:** Implement regional sales strategies to achieve targets for professional customers.
- **Customer Infrastructure:** Ensure robust customer infrastructure, optimize quantity & quality of customers, and no service gaps. Monitor financial health of customers.
- **Distributor Networks:** Set up reseller & **distributor networks** within the territory.
- **Stakeholder Management:** Build relationships with key channel partners.
- **Territory Expansion:** Identify and onboard new customers and distribution partners.
- **Market Insights:** Conduct market research to identify opportunities.
- **Sales Reporting:** Maintain accurate sales records and analyze trends.
- **Team Leadership:** Manage and lead a team of **Territory Sales Officers**, ensuring they meet targets.
- **Financial Hygiene:** Ensure system hygiene at customers (sales returns, damage/shortage claims, stock mismatches, trade discounts).
