# JustMarkets - Partner Relationship Manager

## Company Overview

JustMarkets is a financial services company operating in West Africa, focused on partnership development and business growth in the financial services sector.

## Position Overview

**Role**: Partner Relationship Manager  
**Company**: JustMarkets  
**Location**: West Africa  
**Employment Type**: Full-time  
**Industry**: Financial Services  

## Job Purpose

The Partner Relationship Manager is responsible for driving business growth through strategic partnership acquisition, management, and expansion. This role bridges internal operations with external partners, ensuring mutual success, compliance, and alignment with company objectives.

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Partner Acquisition & Onboarding**
   - Proactively identify and acquire new partnership opportunities
   - Develop onboarding processes for new partners
   - Create partnership agreements and operational frameworks
   - Establish relationships with high-potential partners

2. **Partner Relationship Management**
   - Act as primary contact for partner portfolio
   - Address partner needs and resolve issues promptly
   - Provide strategic guidance and business support
   - Nurture and expand existing relationships
   - Ensure partner satisfaction and business objectives achievement

3. **Compliance & Regulatory Management**
   - Ensure partners operate within business, regulatory, and brand guidelines
   - Maintain highest compliance standards
   - Monitor partner adherence to regulatory requirements
   - Manage compliance documentation and reporting
   - Conduct regulatory training and updates

4. **Market Development & Growth Strategy**
   - Collaborate with marketing and events teams
   - Develop and implement market development strategies
   - Drive partner engagement and growth initiatives
   - Represent company at industry events (expos, conventions, seminars)
   - Occasional short-term travel to West Africa markets

5. **Business Development & Retention**
   - Retain existing partner business through strategic support
   - Provide commercial insights and strategic recommendations
   - Help partners reach new levels of success
   - Identify expansion opportunities within existing partnerships
   - Drive competitive positioning and market growth

6. **KPI Achievement & Reporting**
   - Meet and exceed management-set KPIs
   - Drive business growth and surpass targets
   - Track partnership performance metrics
   - Report on partnership pipeline and conversion rates
   - Monitor market development impact

## Key Performance Indicators

1. **New Partner Acquisition**: Number and quality of new partners onboarded quarterly
2. **Partner Retention Rate**: Percentage of partners retained and growing
3. **Compliance Rate**: 100% partner compliance with regulatory and brand standards
4. **Revenue Growth**: Partner-driven revenue growth and expansion
5. **Satisfaction**: Partner satisfaction scores and feedback
6. **Market Coverage**: Geographic and sector expansion in West Africa
