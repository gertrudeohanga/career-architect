# Business Development Manager, East Africa

## Company Overview
RateHawk is part of a travel-tech company (including ZenHotels and Roundtrip) focused on B2B travel services for tour operators, travel agencies, and OTAs.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and implement sales activities and hit sales targets to achieve growth and expand presence in the assigned territory
- Sign up new partners (tour operators, travel agencies, travel management companies, OTAs) and provide them with extended training on the system
- Build and maintain strong, long-lasting relationships with new and existing partners
- Manage an assigned portfolio of partners, including providing the first level of support
- Follow-up with existing partners to provide system training and identify ways to improve cooperation
- Prepare, execute, and manage commercial deals with partners, including document flow and technical setup
- Control partner compliance with commercial conditions, including payment flow
- Spend approximately 30% of working time on business trips (meetings, industry events, etc.)
- Provide market and competitive environment analysis
- Provide regular comprehensive reporting through CRM and internal systems
- Initiate API deals with potential and existing partners

## Required Qualifications & Experience

- Minimum 3 years of successful experience in the full sales life cycle within the travel industry
- Focus on business development and account management for B2B companies
- In-depth understanding of the region's travel market, including B2B travel networks
- Proficient in Swahili and English
- Metrics-driven and comfortable with data-driven decision-making
- Proactive, ambitious, motivated, action-oriented, results-focused
- Comfortable with a fast-changing environment
- International mindset and ability to work across diverse cultural contexts
- Must be based in Kenya
- API technology deals competence is a plus

## Benefits

- Flexible schedules and remote work opportunities
- Internal programs for soft skills, leadership development, and adaptation
- Partial compensation for external training and conferences
- Corporate English school access
- Corporate prices on hotels and travel services
- MyTime Day Off: An extra non-working day without loss of compensation
