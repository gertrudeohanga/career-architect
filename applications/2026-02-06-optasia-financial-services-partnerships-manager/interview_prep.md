# Interview Preparation

## Company Research
- **Name:** Optasia (formerly Channel VAS).
- **What they do:** AI-led Fintech platform. They provide "Airtime Credit Services" and "Micro-Lending" solutions to Mobile Network Operators (MNOs) and Banks.
- **Key Product:** Providing credit/loans to unbanked people using alternative data (scoring).
- **Partners:** Safaricom, Airtell, Banks.
- **Mission:** Financial Inclusion.

## Likely Interview Questions

### Partnership & Operations
**Q: "How do you manage a relationship with a bank partner?"**
*Answer:* "Transparency and Reliability.
1.  **Regular Cadence:** Weekly operational calls, Monthly strategic reviews.
2.  **Data-Driven:** Always bring the numbers (Disursement volume, NPLs, Repayment rates).
3.  **Proactive Problem Solving:** If a system is down or scoring looks off, I alert them before they call me."

### Fintech Ecosystem
**Q: "How does Optasia fit into the ecosystem?"**
*Answer:* "Optasia sits in the middle (B2B2X). You connect the **Capital Provider** (Bank) with the **Distributor** (Telco/Wallet) using your **Scoring Engine**. My role is to make sure the Capital Provider (FSP) is happy, compliant, and seeing growth."

**Q: "Tell us about a time you resolved an operational issue."**
*Answer:* "At Kwendo, we had a client whose onboarding was delayed due to documentation issues. I set up a direct line between their compliance team and our internal ops team, created a shared checklist, and held daily 15-min standups until it was resolved. We launched only 2 days late instead of 2 weeks."

## Technical Awareness (The "Bonus" Skills)
