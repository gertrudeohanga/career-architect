# Customer Service Team Leader

## Job Purpose
Lead and coach a high-performing team of Customer Service Representatives to deliver fast, consistent, and exceptional support across all customer channels. Drive service excellence, operational efficiency, and adherence to performance standards in a high-volume, shift-based environment.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Team Leadership:** Lead consistent high-quality CX across Voice, Chat, Email, WhatsApp, Social Media.
- **Escalation Management:** Act as point of contact for complex issues and guide team resolution.
- **Performance Monitoring:** Monitor real-time dashboards/queues and guide team to hit daily KPIs.
- **Coaching & QA:** Conduct regular coaching, QA reviews, and performance check-ins.
- **Process Improvement:** Identify trends in queries and recommend changes to SOPs/Scripts.
- **Shift Management:** Lead daily pre-shift briefings and align team on targets.
- **Culture:** Foster a culture of excellence, accountability, and "Tujienjoy kazini".

## Requirements
- **Experience:**
    - 2–3 years in fast-paced inbound customer service.
    - **Minimum 1 year in a Team Leadership role.**
    - Experience managing shift-based teams of 5–12 agents.
- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree or Diploma in Communications, PR, or relevant field.
- **Skills:**
    - Strong digital literacy: CRM, Workforce Management Systems.
    - Strong team leadership and coaching capability.
    - Fluent in **Kiswahili and English**.
    - Flexibility to work rotating shifts (evenings, weekends, holidays).
