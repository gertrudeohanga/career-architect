# Call Center Officer

## Job Summary
Oya Microcredit (Fast-growing MFI) seeks a proactive **Call Center Officer** to handle inbound/outbound calls, provide loan information, and support collections.

## Key Responsibilities
-   **Call Handling:** Inbound and outbound calls; professional and courteous.
-   **Product Info:** Provide accurate info on loan products, repayment schedules, and policies.
-   **Follow-up:** Call clients regarding loan applications, documentation, and **repayments**.
-   **Records:** Maintain proper records of all interactions (CRM/System).
-   **Targets:** Meet daily/weekly call targets and KPIs.
-   **Collections:** Support the collections and customer retention process.

## Qualifications
-   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, Economics, Marketing, or related.
-   **Experience:** 1–3 years in a **Call Center** or **Customer Service** role (**Financial Institution/Microcredit** added advantage).
-   **Skills:** Communication, Data Capture, Multitasking, Problem-Solving.
-   **Tech:** MS Office, CRM systems.

## Key Competencies
-   Customer Focus
-   Integrity
-   Active Listening
-   Adaptability
