# Momentum Credit - Sales Executive – Microfinance

## Company Overview

Momentum Credit was founded on July 29, 2013, and was formerly known as Platinum Bridging Capital Limited. The company rebranded to Momentum Credit in March 2017 with purposeful intent to reorient its focus to meet an overwhelming market need of providing structured working capital solutions to Individuals and Small Medium Enterprises (SMEs). The company operates in Kenya's microfinance sector, focusing on bridging capital and working capital financing for underserved market segments.

## Role Summary

The Sales Executive – Microfinance is responsible for driving sales growth through new customer acquisition and existing account management. This role requires a results-oriented professional who can meet and exceed sales targets, manage complex negotiations with senior-level executives, and build long-term customer relationships in the microfinance and SME lending space.

## Key Responsibilities

### Sales Performance
- Meet and exceed sales targets consistently
- Successfully create business from new and existing customer accounts
- Drive revenue growth through strategic customer acquisition

### Relationship Management
- Build rapport and establish long-term relationships with customers
- Manage complex negotiations with senior-level executives
- Maintain high customer satisfaction and retention rates

### Account Management
- Develop and manage customer accounts across individual and SME segments
- Identify cross-selling and upselling opportunities
- Provide structured working capital solutions tailored to customer needs

## Required Qualifications

### Experience
- 2-5 years' quota carrying sales experience
- Demonstrable track record of over-achieving quota
- Experience in microfinance, lending, or financial services (preferred)

### Technical Skills
- Experience and working knowledge of CRM systems
- Proficiency in sales pipeline management
- Understanding of working capital solutions and SME financing

### Soft Skills
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Excellent negotiation and relationship-building abilities
- Results-oriented with proven ability to exceed targets
- Customer-centric approach
