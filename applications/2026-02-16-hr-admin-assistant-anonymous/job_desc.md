# HR & Admin Assistant

## Job Summary
Operational support in **Human Resource Management** and **General Office Administration**. Organized, proactive candidate to support people management and day-to-day admin.

## Key Responsibilities
1.  **Recruitment & Onboarding:**
    -   Assist in advertising, shortlisting, and interview coordination.
    -   Prepare **employment contracts** and onboarding docs.
    -   Coordinate induction/orientation.
    -   Track probation and renewals.
2.  **HR Administration:**
    -   Maintain employee records (physical/digital).
    -   Monitor **attendance, leave records, shift schedules**.
    -   Ensure compliance with labour laws.
    -   Coordinate meetings/minutes.
3.  **Employee Relations:**
    -   First point of contact for HR inquiries.
    -   Support disciplinary processes.
    -   Staff welfare/engagement.
4.  **HR Reporting:**
    -   Update dashboards (headcount, turnover).
    -   Weekly/Monthly reports.
