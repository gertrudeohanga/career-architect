# Interview Preparation Guide: iPOS Field Sales Agent

## Company & Role Understanding

### Enduring Technologies & iPOS Platform
- **Company**: Software company specializing in mobile point-of-sale (mPOS) solutions
- **iPOS Platform**: Mobile POS solution for Agrodealers and SMEs in Kenya
- **Mission**: Digitize operations for businesses, streamline sales tracking, inventory management, and forecasting
- **Target Markets**: Agricultural sector (Agrodealers), Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs)

### Role Priorities
1. **Customer Acquisition**: Identify and engage potential iPOS customers in assigned geographic area
2. **Customer Onboarding**: Set up platform on devices, train customers, ensure smooth adoption
3. **Customer Support**: Provide technical support, troubleshooting, and ongoing assistance
4. **Relationship Management**: Build strong relationships, maintain high retention rates
5. **Field Operations**: Travel within assigned county, conduct regular customer visits

## Likely Interview Questions & Responses

### **Field Sales & Customer Acquisition**

**Q1: "Describe your experience in field sales and customer acquisition."**

**Key Points:**
- 5+ years field sales experience across financial services
- Direct customer engagement and prospecting
- Consistent target achievement and customer acquisition
- Experience working across diverse Kenyan markets

**Sample Response:**
"I have 5+ years of field sales experience across financial services sectors. At Ngao Credit Limited, I conducted direct field sales, promoting logbook loans and guiding clients through complete application processes. I exceeded monthly sales targets through systematic lead generation and follow-up strategies. At Kwendo, I led business development initiatives expanding market presence across African businesses, conducting client outreach and building relationships with key decision-makers."

**Q2: "How would you approach identifying and engaging potential iPOS customers?"**

**Key Points:**
- Systematic approach to prospecting
- Understanding customer needs and pain points
- Explaining iPOS benefits clearly
- Building relationships before selling

**Sample Response:**
"I would approach customer identification systematically by researching local businesses, understanding their current operations, and identifying pain points that iPOS can solve. I would engage potential customers by first building relationships and understanding their challenges, then explaining how iPOS can specifically benefit their business through improved efficiency, better inventory management, and enhanced business visibility. I believe in consultative selling rather than aggressive pitching."

### **Customer Training & Onboarding**

**Q3: "Describe your experience training customers on new technology or software."**

**Key Points:**
- Customer training experience with digital platforms
- Ability to explain complex concepts simply
- Patience working with non-technical users
- Hands-on training approach

**Sample Response:**
"I have extensive experience training customers on new technology. At Schmsoft Ventures, I provided staff training on customer service excellence and product knowledge. At Kwendo, I trained clients on digital payroll platform features and benefits. I believe in hands-on training, breaking down complex concepts into simple steps, and being patient with customers who may not be technically savvy. I ensure customers feel comfortable and confident using new systems."

**Q4: "How would you handle a customer who is struggling to learn the iPOS platform?"**

**Key Points:**
- Patience and empathy
- Step-by-step approach
- Multiple training methods
- Follow-up support

**Sample Response:**
"I would approach struggling customers with patience and empathy, understanding that technology adoption can be challenging. I would break down the training into smaller, manageable steps, use multiple teaching methods including hands-on practice, and provide clear written instructions. I would schedule follow-up visits to reinforce learning and be available for questions between visits. The key is ensuring the customer feels supported rather than pressured."

### **Technical Support & Troubleshooting**

**Q5: "Describe your technical support experience."**

**Key Points:**
- Help desk experience
- Mobile technology proficiency
- Systematic troubleshooting approach
- Problem-solving skills

**Sample Response:**
"I have technical support experience from my role at Code Particles, where I served as first-line technical support. I performed remote troubleshooting using diagnostic techniques and guided customers through problem-solving processes. I'm proficient with mobile technology and have experience with mobile banking platforms. I take a systematic approach to troubleshooting, identifying the root cause and providing clear, step-by-step solutions."

**Q6: "How would you handle a technical issue you couldn't resolve?"**

**Key Points:**
- Escalation process
- Clear communication
- Learning from experience
- Customer management

**Sample Response:**
"If I encountered a technical issue I couldn't resolve, I would first ensure the customer understands I'm working to help them. I would document the issue clearly, escalate it to the appropriate technical team while providing all relevant details, and follow up with the customer until the issue is resolved. I would also learn from the experience to build my technical knowledge for future issues."

### **Field Work & Geographic Adaptation**

I've researched the area and understand it has a vibrant business environment that would benefit from iPOS digitization solutions. However, I'm adaptable and confident I could succeed in any of the listed counties given my experience working across diverse Kenyan markets."

### **Customer Relationship Management**

**Q9: "How do you maintain strong customer relationships and ensure high retention?"**

**Key Points:**

- Understanding customer needs
- Proactive support
- Building trust and rapport

**Sample Response:**

At Schmsoft Ventures, I built long-term client relationships that enhanced service delivery quality, and I focus on being a trusted advisor rather than just a sales person."

**Q10: "How would you handle customer complaints or difficult situations?"**

**Key Points:**
- Professional and empathetic approach
- Problem-solving focus
- Follow-up until resolution
- Learning from feedback

**Sample Response:**
"I handle customer complaints professionally and empathetically, first listening carefully to understand their concerns. I focus on finding solutions rather than making excuses, and I follow up until the issue is fully resolved to their satisfaction. I also view complaints as learning opportunities to improve our service and prevent similar issues in the future."
