# Enduring Technologies - iPOS Field Sales Agent

## Company Overview
**Enduring Technologies Limited (ETL)** - Software company specializing in mobile point-of-sale (mPOS) solutions. At the heart of ETL is iPOS - a platform designed to digitize operations for Agrodealers and SMEs in Kenya, streamlining sales tracking, inventory management, and business forecasting.

## Product & Mission
**iPOS Platform**: Mobile point-of-sale solution prioritizing mobility, affordability, and enhanced visibility for business owners. Designed to empower MSMEs through POS software tailored to the local business environment, particularly in the agricultural sector.

## Role Details
**Position**: iPOS Field Sales Agent (Various Counties)  
**Location**: Multiple counties (Nyeri, Meru, Trans Nzoia, Homa Bay, Tharaka Nithi, Nandi, Bomet, Mbarara-Uganda)  
**Contract**: Full time  
**Education**: Diploma, Associate's degree (Bachelor's preferred)  
**Experience**: 6 months minimum  
**Language**: English, Swahili  
**Apply by**: February 28, 2026

## Primary Objective

The primary objective of the iPOS Field Sales Agent is to onboard, train and maintain customers within the iPOS platform while ensuring timely and effective support, including software set up, installation, training and troubleshooting.

## Key Responsibilities

### **Customer Acquisition & Onboarding**
- Identify and engage potential iPOS customers in assigned geographic area
- Explain the benefits of iPOS to new customers and encourage subscription
- Accurately profile new customers and minimize customer turnover
- Ensure smooth onboarding of new customers, including follow-ups to support ongoing usage
- Set up iPOS platform on customer devices and train them on effective usage

### **Relationship Management & Support**
- Provide excellent customer support through regular visits and account maintenance
- Build strong relationships with customers through regular communication and support
- Understand customer needs and provide timely solutions to ensure satisfaction
- Strive to maintain high customer retention by addressing needs proactively
- Follow up on complaints until they are resolved

### **Technical Support & Troubleshooting**
- Respond promptly to technical issues and ensure efficient resolution
- Set up iPOS platform on customer devices and provide training
- Troubleshoot software and technical problems for customers
- Collaborate with other iPOS teams to ensure customer success

### **Partnership & Business Development**
- Work with partners and stakeholders in the value chain to create strong customer pipeline
- Work with partners and stakeholders to upsell their products and services
- Liaise with iPOS Team to implement new initiatives and improve business practices
- Keep clear and detailed reports on activities, progress, and challenges

### **Reporting & Administration**
- Report information as guided by supervisor
- Maintain confidentiality of all information
- Perform other duties as needed
- Support ETL's values and diversity initiatives
