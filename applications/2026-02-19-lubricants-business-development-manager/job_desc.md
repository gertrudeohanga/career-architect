# Lubricants Company - Business Development Manager

## Company Overview

A lubricants sector company operating in Kenya, focused on B2B partnerships within the industrial and commercial lubricants market. The company works with distributors and corporate clients to expand market presence across key industrial segments.

## Role Summary

The Business Development Manager will drive growth and expand B2B partnerships within the lubricants sector in Kenya. This role focuses on identifying new business opportunities, strengthening relationships with key industrial and commercial customers, and developing sustainable revenue streams. The position requires working closely with distributors, corporate clients, and internal teams to grow market presence and deliver strong commercial results.

## Key Responsibilities

- Drive growth and expand B2B partnerships within the lubricants sector
- Identify new business opportunities in industrial and commercial segments
- Strengthen relationships with key industrial and commercial customers
- Develop sustainable revenue streams
- Work closely with distributors and corporate clients
- Build sales pipelines and manage key accounts
- Negotiate contracts with industrial customers
- Monitor market trends in the lubricants sector
- Ensure effective execution of business strategies across key segments
- Grow market presence and deliver strong commercial results

## Required Qualifications

- 10+ years of professional experience
- Strong exposure to B2B sales and business development
- Proven ability to develop new markets
- Experience managing key accounts
- Strong commercial acumen
- Solid negotiation skills
- Strong relationship-building skills
- Experience working with distributors
- Experience working with industrial customers
- Results-driven and proactive
- Able to work independently
- Excellent communication capabilities
- Strong stakeholder management capabilities

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in the lubricants sector
- Knowledge of industrial and commercial customer segments
- Understanding of distributor management
- Track record of expanding market footprint
