# Kaziweza Limited - Regional Manager - Fashion Retail

## Company Overview

Kaziweza Limited operates fashion retail stores across East Africa, with operations in Uganda and Rwanda. The company focuses on brand integrity, customer experience excellence, and operational efficiency across multiple retail locations. The organization follows structured Operating Standards across five core pillars: Revenue & Profit, People, Product, Brand Presentation, and Customer Experience.

## Role Summary

The Regional Manager is responsible for driving the commercial success, operational efficiency, and brand integrity of all stores across Uganda and Rwanda. This role oversees full regional performance, ensuring alignment to Operating Standards, managing external relationships with suppliers and landlords, and serving as the primary point of contact for Fashion Group operations in both countries. The position requires frequent travel between Kampala (base) and Kigali.

## Key Responsibilities

**Regional Revenue & Profitability**
- Drive achievement of all country-based targets against retail KPIs including conversion, basket size, and MSI
- Develop and implement sales strategies to achieve revenue targets for the region
- Implement corrective action to address performance gaps

**Marketing & Partnerships**
- Share local market insights and competitor trends with HQ
- Advise HQ on country-specific opportunities and risks
- Coordinate country-specific marketing campaigns aligned with overall company strategy
- Build relationships with key stakeholders, media, community partners, and local influencers
- Ensure execution of regional marketing campaigns and promotions aligned to brand plans

**Stakeholder Management**
- Manage relationships with landlords, local suppliers, logistics providers, and regulators
- Represent the company in key business forums and negotiations in both markets

**People Management & Leadership**
- Lead, coach, and mentor Store Managers within the region to meet sales and customer experience objectives
- Develop staffing plans and allocate resources to ensure optimal operations
- Monitor staff performance and provide ongoing feedback, training, and development opportunities
- Make recommendations for recruitment and performance management for all regional staff

**Product Performance**
- Serve as key point of contact between regional stores and HQ
- Monitor product performance and stock levels
- Coordinate with merchandising team to ensure assortment balance and stock optimization
- Escalate product-related issues to relevant HQ teams and follow up for resolution

**Customer Experience Excellence & VM Standards**
- Drive customer experience strategy across all stores in the region
- Implement and monitor customer feedback systems to identify improvement areas
- Ensure stores maintain Visual Merchandising standards in accordance with brand guidelines

**Stock Control and Asset Management**
- Oversee stock movement activities and management practices across all regional stores
- Ensure all operating requirements are met
- Implement loss prevention strategies to minimize stock losses and shrinkage

**Regional Operational Efficiency**
- Ensure each store operates in accordance with company operating standards
- Ensure compliance with operational policies and procedures
- Conduct regular store visits to assess operations, merchandising, and staff performance

**Reporting & Analysis**
- Track key metrics and prepare monthly performance reports
- Highlight achievements, challenges, and action plans
- Analyze sales data and customer feedback to inform strategic decision-making

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Fashion, Marketing, Business Management, or related field
- 5+ years of leadership experience managing multi-store or multi-country operations in the Fashion Retail Industry
- Demonstrated ability to manage performance across multiple KPIs
- Strong commercial acumen and operational discipline
- Excellent communicator and people leader
- Skilled in stakeholder engagement and market representation
- Experience in fashion retail, brand execution, and visual merchandising
- Fluent in English (French or Swahili a plus)
- Willing to travel frequently between Uganda and Rwanda
