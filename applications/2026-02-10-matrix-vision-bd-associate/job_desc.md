# Business Development Associate – Corporate Software Sales

## Description
Matrix Visions Systems is a leading technology company providing engineering, security, and software-integrated solutions. We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Business Development Associate to spearhead growth in our **corporate software sales division**.

## Responsibilities
- **Sales Strategy:** Develop and execute sales strategies to penetrate corporate accounts.
- **New Business:** Identify, qualify, and close new business opportunities in **enterprise software solutions**.
- **Public Procurement:** Mine opportunities on **EGP, MyGov** and other Public Procurement sites.
- **Relationship Management:** Build relationships with C-level executives, IT decision-makers.
- **Consultative Selling:** Conduct needs assessments and deliver tailored software solutions.
- **Market Development:** Analyze trends, competitor offerings, and emerging tech.
- **Collaboration:** Work with pre-sales, technical, and customer success teams.

## Qualifications
- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Business, IT, or related field.
- **Experience:** **3+ years** in enterprise software sales or business development.
- **Track Record:** Proven ability to meet/exceed sales targets in B2B environments.
- **Tech Skills:** Strong understanding of corporate IT ecosystems (**ERP, CRM, cloud, SaaS**).
- **Public Sector:** Strong understanding of EGP and the Public procurement cycle.

## Key Competencies
- Consultative selling and solution-based approaches.
- Strong networking and relationship-building abilities.
- Comfortable working in fast-paced, target-driven environments.
