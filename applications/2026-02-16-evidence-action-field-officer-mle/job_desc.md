# Field Officer, MLE (Temporary)

## About the Role
Ensure the **Safe Water Now** program delivers impact through high-quality **Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E)**. Collect accurate data on **Dispensers for Safe Water (DSW)**, maintain stakeholder relationships, and provide field insights.

## Responsibilities
-   **Data Collection:** Conduct monitoring via questionnaire, water sample collection, and observation using **electronic details (ODK/SurveyCTO)**.
-   **Quality:** Participation in bench-testing tools; ensure data flow protocols.
-   **Stakeholder:** Build relationships with local government and community leaders (Chiefs, Village Elders).
-   **Communication:** Provide daily field insights; flag emerging trends.
-   **Financial:** Manage field-level transaction receipts.

## Requirements
-   **Education:** Minimum Diploma in **Social Science** (Public Health, Community Health, Project Management, etc.).
-   **Experience:** 1+ year in **research**, **survey administration**, **community engagement**, or NGO work.
-   **Tech:** Experience with **ODK / SurveyCTO / KOBO**.
-   **Languages:** English & Kiswahili (Luo/Luhya added advantage).
-   **Logistics:** Valid Personal Accident Insurance & ID. **Motorcycle** travel required.

## Locations
Mumias, Chavakali, Oyugis, Ndhiwa, Awendo.
