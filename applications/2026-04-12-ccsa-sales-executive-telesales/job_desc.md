# Call Center Solutions Africa (CCSA) - Sales Executive – Telesales & Omnichannel Growth

## Company Overview

Call Center Solutions Africa (CCSA) is a fast-growing outsourced sales and customer engagement company serving businesses across Africa, Europe, and global markets. The company specializes in:
- High-performance telesales
- Lead conversion
- Omnichannel outreach
- Revenue generation for clients

CCSA is building a world-class sales engine focused on targets, conversations, and closing deals across multiple channels.

## Role Summary

The Sales Executive – Telesales & Omnichannel Growth is a frontline revenue generation role for experienced sales professionals who thrive on targets and closing deals. This position focuses on high-volume outbound telesales campaigns, omnichannel outreach (phone, email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn), and converting warm and cold leads into paying customers. The role requires strong English fluency, persuasion skills, and comfort with performance-based metrics.

## Key Responsibilities

### Telesales & Outbound Campaigns
- Execute high-volume outbound telesales campaigns targeting qualified leads provided via CRM
- Engage prospects through omnichannel outreach (calls, email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn)
- Spend most of the day on calls and follow-ups

### Sales Process & Conversion
- Conduct needs discovery and qualify prospects
- Confidently pitch client solutions
- Handle objections and close deals while maintaining high conversion rates
- Follow structured sales processes while thinking independently

### Performance & CRM Management
- Accurately update CRM systems with call outcomes, notes, and next actions
- Meet and exceed daily, weekly, and monthly sales targets
- Track performance against clear KPIs

### Collaboration & Improvement
- Collaborate with QA, training, and team leads to continuously improve scripts and performance
- Maintain exceptional professionalism, tone, and clarity in all customer interactions

## Required Qualifications

### Experience
- **Minimum**: 2 years proven experience in telesales, inside sales, or call center sales
- Experience working with CRMs and sales dialers
- Ability to follow structured sales processes

### Skills & Competencies
- **MANDATORY**: Native-level or near-native English fluency (spoken and written)
- Strong persuasion, negotiation, and closing skills
- High energy, resilience, and comfort with rejection
- Confident speaking to decision-makers across different industries

### Ideal Candidate Profile
- A hunter, not an order-taker
- Comfortable spending most of day on calls and follow-ups
- Target-driven and motivated by performance-based earnings
- Coachable, competitive, and eager to grow
- Ready to sell, not just talk about sales

## Compensation & Benefits

- **Salary Range**: KES 75,000 - 145,000 per year (estimated)
- **Structure**: Competitive base salary + uncapped commissions
- **Performance-based**: Earnings tied to results

### Additional Benefits
- Clear KPIs and transparent performance tracking
- Structured onboarding, sales playbooks, and ongoing coaching
- Exposure to international clients and markets
- Fast growth opportunities into Senior Sales, Team Lead, or Account Manager roles
- Performance-driven, ambitious sales culture
