# Q-Sourcing Limited - Business Development and Operations Manager

## Company Overview

Q-Sourcing Limited trading as Q-Sourcing Servtec is a manpower management solutions firm operating in the East African Region in the countries of Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and South Sudan. The company provides comprehensive human resource and manpower management solutions across the region.

## Role Summary

The Business Development and Operations Manager will be responsible for driving business growth through lead generation and marketing, managing client relationships, leading and mentoring the team, and overseeing general business operations. This role is being recruited on behalf of a client in the tours and travel industry in Nairobi, Kenya.

## Key Responsibilities

- Business Development and Lead generation through different marketing channels
- Offer clients superior Customer experience by providing solutions to meet their needs
- Manage client relationships and partnerships to create lasting business relationships
- Lead people by coaching and mentoring the team
- Manage general business operations

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Tours and travel, sales and marketing or related field
- Minimum 3 years of experience in corporate sales, corporate relations management, public relations, or a similar role in a tours and travel company
- Understanding of tours and travel industry operations
- Thorough understanding of marketing/sales and negotiation techniques
- Self-motivated and results driven attitude
