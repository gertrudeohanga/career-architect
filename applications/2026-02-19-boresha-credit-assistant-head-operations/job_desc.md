# Boresha Credit Services Limited - Assistant Head of Operations

## Company Overview

Boresha Credit Services Limited (BCSL) is a leading debt collection firm in Kenya and East Africa. The company specializes in debt recovery services, collections management, and credit risk mitigation for financial institutions and corporate clients. BCSL operates in the banking, finance, and insurance sector with a focus on operational excellence and collections performance.

## Role Summary

Assistant Head of Operations responsible for supporting the implementation of operational strategies, monitoring team performance, and ensuring collections targets are met. This role reports directly to the Head of Operations and serves as the operational point of contact in their absence. The position requires strong operational management, team leadership, performance monitoring, reporting, and collections expertise. This is a contract position based in Nairobi.

## Key Responsibilities

**Operational Strategy & Performance Management**
- Support implementation of operational strategies, KPIs, and collections targets set by the Head of Operations
- Monitor day-to-day team performance, call quality, attendance, and productivity against company standards
- Track collections performance, outstanding balances, and escalate gaps or risks for management action
- Support system updates, data accuracy, and operational process improvements

**Team Leadership & Development**
- Conduct regular team meetings, coaching sessions, and one-on-one follow-ups with Team Leaders and ACMs (Account Collection Managers)
- Enforce adherence to company policies, procedures, and code of conduct at team level
- Maintain accurate attendance, leave records, and support staffing coordination

**Reporting & Documentation**
- Compile, review, and submit operational, performance, and client reports within stipulated timelines
- Ensure timely sharing of client performance reports, payment confirmations, and invoices
- Track collections performance and outstanding balances for management reporting

**Collections & Financial Management**
- Follow up on missing payments and support reconciliation and invoicing processes
- Monitor collections targets and performance metrics
- Escalate payment gaps or risks for management action

**Acting Leadership**
- Act as the operational point of contact in the absence of the Head of Operations
- Perform any other duties assigned by the Head of Operations

## Required Qualifications

**Education & Experience (Either/Or)**:
- Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 1 year of experience in debt collections, OR
- Diploma in a relevant field with minimum 3 years' experience in debt collection

**Technical Skills**:
- Excellent knowledge of working with customer support and/or customer information systems
- Strong analytical and negotiation skills
- Good problem-solving skills

**Soft Skills & Competencies**:
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Fluency in both spoken and written English is a must
- Keen eye for detail and ability to identify discrepancies or hidden debtor information
- High level of discretion and ability to work with confidential information
- Ability to work within tight timelines
- Team player

## Preferred Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Operations Management, or related field
- Experience in financial services or banking sector
- Experience with collections management systems
- Leadership or supervisory experience
- Knowledge of debt collection regulations and compliance

## Tech Stack / Tools

- **Customer Information Systems:** Collections management systems, CRM platforms
- **Reporting Tools:** MS Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint), reporting dashboards
- **Communication:** Email, phone systems, customer support platforms
- **Other:** Attendance tracking systems, invoicing systems
