# Senior Manager - Credit Analysis

## Job Purpose
To manage the credit risk analysis function and preparation of credit proposals. Liaison with RMs and Branch Managers. Formulate and implement credit analysis policies.

## Key Responsibilities
1.  **Credit Analysis Management:**
    -   Supervise Credit Analysts.
    -   Financial evaluation of new business/projects.
    -   Decline non-viable deals at source.
    -   Analyze and structure **SME, Corporate and Trade Finance** facility requests.
    -   Credit grading of borrowers.
2.  **Credit Risk Monitoring:**
    -   Climate Risk, E&S Risk, Reputational Risk assessment.
    -   Track covenants & risk triggers.
    -   Identify accounts showing early signs of deterioration.
3.  **Customer Experience:**
    -   Ensure TAT adherence.
    -   Reduce rework on credit papers.
4.  **Leadership:**
    -   Set performance objectives for the team.
    -   Succession planning and training.
