# Credit Sales Officer

## Description
As a Credit Sales Officer (CSO) of AZIMA, you will play a significant operational development role in contributing to shaping the growth and success of AZIMA. As CSO you will use your Credit Sales knowledge, acumen and experience to make credit sales whilst filtering credit sales prospects, and helping develop and implement Credit Sales policies, procedures and practices.

## Responsibilities
- **Assist in growing the Credit Sales Function.**
- **Optimising Credit Sales Functions CSFs/KPIs.**
- **Executing Credit Sales strategies** to achieve short and long-term goals.
- Implementing Credit Sales plans to **optimise cost-efficiency**.
- Maintaining positive, ethical and trust based relations with key stakeholders.
- Performing Credit Sales operations in compliance with ethical, legal and regulatory obligations.
- Assisting or guiding and collaborating with other Credit Sales staff.
- Assist in the **recovery and collection** of outstanding fees/ arrears and late payment fees.
- Please note: Azima is a **start-up microfinance money lender** providing services to the underbanked.
